12. Buatlah recount text tentang Museum harus ada keterang/bagian seperti Orientation, Event, dan Reorientation #mohon dibatu!!! harus menggunakan bahasa inggris


Sangiran Museum

Last month, my family and I visited Sangiran museum, one of the historical sites in Indoneisa. It is located in the north of Surakarta. We went there by car. ( orientation )
On the way to Sangiran Museum, we saw many notices showing the sites of fossil discoveries. The visitors could also see the view around the sites from the tower on the top of a hill. A long the road, there are many souvenir shops. They mostly provide unique and antique souvenirs made of stones.After arriving at the gateway of the Sangiran Museum, we bought entrance tickets and parked the car. Then, we walked to the museum. There are two main buildings which are connected by a small long corridor.In the first building, we saw many fossils dealing with prehistoric men. There we could learn Darwin's theory of evolution and see the miniaturs of humans evolution  according to Darwin theory. We could also see the equipment used by prehistoric men to live and search food. Besides, we also saw a diorama showing miniatures of prehistoric men and their living. We could take photographs on the miniatures, but we were not allowed to touch them. In that place we felt as if we lived in prehistoric ages. After that, we went to next building through the corridor. the museum is located in a high area. Therefore, we could see the view of the valley below the museum from the corridor. ( Event )Finally we reached the second building. In the building we could see many animals fossils. such as the fossils of a very big elephants tusk, a crocodile's jaw, some other animals skeleton and many more. We could also see a map showing the sites of fossils discoveries. ( Re-orientation )

15. Biography pahlawan dalam bahasa inggris , namun singkat dan jelas sperti poin2 saja orientasi event reorientasi


When she was 12 years old, she had to quit studying because she had to do “pingit” at that time, a rule of Javanese custom that forbid woman to go outside before marriage. Finally, on 12th November 1983 she was married to Raden Adipati Joyodiningrat.

After getting married, R.A Kartini never stopped learning. She continued to study in her own house by reading a lot of European books and magazines. When she was reading them, she found a great difference between European women’s life with Indonesian women. European women live in freedom and they have the same equivalent position with men. While, Indonesia women lived under man’s power.

Based on that fact, she had an idea to change Indonesian women’s life. She along with her friends opened the first school for women in Indonesiain 1912 in Semarang. They taught Indonesian women how to read and write, so that they could come out of ignorance.

In addition to teaching, she also wrote letters to her friends in the Netherlands. One of them is Rosa Abendanon which strongly supported Kartini’s idea. She also often wrote to the Dutch women’s magazine De Hollandsche Lelieseveral times.

Thanks to her efforts, many women schools were built in other areas such as in Surabaya, Yogyakarta, Malang, Madiun, and Cirebon. Moreover, there was also Kartini Foundation, founded by Van Deventer’s family who is a political figures, opened Kartini’s School.

MY Joyful Eid Al-Fitri Holiday

This year-end school holiday is very fun because the school holidays coincide with the Eid al-Fitr holiday. Mom and Dad also get holiday leave, so we can spend more time together than we did in previous years.

We planned to go to grandma's house in Brebes. Dad had booked a train ticket three days before the holiday and a week later. The day we arrived, our family went straight to the station and took the train to Brebes.

Upon arrival at Brebes Station, grandpa had welcomed us with our cousin named Althan. It was great to see grandpa healthy and smiling happily welcoming us. Then grandpa drove us to his house. Arriving home, grandma greeted us with pleasure. Grandma has cooked many of our favorite foods for breaking the fast. Aunt and uncle were also there welcoming us. It's so nice to meet my other cousins.

While at grandma's house, we rarely went outside the house. Because the city of Brebes is very hot and the roads are jammed. To go anywhere, it took us a long time because after the holiday the streets began to be crowded by the backflow. At home, we play sand that can be formed in various shapes and sometimes cycling in the village in the afternoon.

Arti:

Libur akhir tahun pelajaran kali ini sangat menyenangkan karena libur sekolah bersamaan dengan libur Hari Raya Idul Fitri. Ayah dan Ibu juga memperoleh cuti hari raya, sehingga kami dapat menghabiskan waktu bersama-sama lebih lama daripada libur tahun-tahun sebelumnya.

Kami berencana untuk pergi ke rumah nenek di Brebes. Ayah telah memesan tiket kereta api tiga hari sebelum hari raya dan seminggu setelahnya. Hari keberangkatan kami pun tiba, kami sekeluarga langsung pergi ke stasiun dan menaiki kereta menuju Brebes.

Setibanya di Stasiun Brebes, kakek sudah menyambut kami bersama sepupu kami yang bernama Althan. Senang sekali melihat kakek sehat dan tersenyum bahagia menyambut kami. Lalu kakek mengantar kami ke rumahnya. Sesampainya di rumah, nenek menyambut kami dengan senang. Nenek telah memasak banyak makanan kesukaan kami untuk berbuka puasa. Tante dan paman juga ada menyambut kami. Senang sekali dapat bertemu dengan sepupu-sepupuku yang lain.

Selama di rumah nenek, kami jarang pergi ke luar rumah. Karena kota Brebes sangat panas dan jalanan pun macet. Untuk pergi kemana-mana, kami memerlukan waktu yang lama karena setelah hari raya jalanan mulai ramai oleh arus balik. Di rumah, kami bermain pasir yang dapat dibentuk beraneka macam bentuk dan kadang bersepeda di kampung pada sore hari.

22. contoh cerita bahasa Inggris tentang belajar bersama teman dengan menggunakan orientation, event, dan reorientation (beserta terjemahannya)​


Jawaban:

Once upon a time, there were three friends, John, Sarah, and David, who wanted to learn something new together. They decided to use the orientation, event, and reorientation method to help them learn.

First, they had an orientation session. During this session, they discussed what they wanted to learn and how they would go about it. They also discussed the resources they would need and the timeline for their learning.

Next, they had an event. During this event, they worked together to learn the new skill. They discussed the topics they were learning, asked questions, and shared their experiences.

Finally, they had a reorientation session. During this session, they discussed what they had learned and how they could apply it in the future. They also discussed any challenges they faced and how they could overcome them.

Ketika dahulu, ada tiga teman, John, Sarah, dan David, yang ingin belajar sesuatu yang baru bersama-sama. Mereka memutuskan untuk menggunakan metode orientasi, acara, dan reorientasi untuk membantu mereka belajar.

Pertama, mereka mengadakan sesi orientasi. Selama sesi ini, mereka membahas apa yang ingin mereka pelajari dan bagaimana mereka akan melakukannya. Mereka juga membahas sumber daya yang mereka butuhkan dan jadwal belajar mereka.

Selanjutnya, mereka mengadakan sebuah acara. Selama acara ini, mereka bekerja sama untuk belajar keterampilan baru. Mereka membahas topik yang sedang mereka pelajari, bertanya, dan berbagi pengalaman mereka.

Akhirnya, mereka mengadakan sesi reorientasi. Selama sesi ini, mereka membahas apa yang telah mereka pelajari dan bagaimana mereka dapat menerapkannya di masa depan. Mereka juga membahas setiap tantangan yang mereka hadapi dan bagaimana mereka dapat mengatasinya.

25. Buatkan contoh cerita pengalaman pribadi saat hari kemerdekaan dalam bahasa Inggris dengan orientation, event,reorientation ​


Jawaban:

Orientation:

On a scorching August day in my hometown, I woke up with a sense of excitement in the air. It was Independence Day, a day filled with patriotism and celebrations.

Event:

I joined my friends and family as we headed to the local park, where the main festivities were scheduled to take place. The park was adorned with flags, banners, and colorful decorations, all symbolizing our nation's freedom.

As we arrived, the national anthem began playing, and everyone stood proudly, singing along with heartfelt enthusiasm. The sound of the anthem echoed through the park, creating a sense of unity among the diverse crowd.

After the anthem, there were various cultural performances, showcasing the rich diversity of our country. Dancers in traditional attire swayed to folk music, and there were even dramatic reenactments of significant moments from our history.

The highlight of the event was the spectacular fireworks display that lit up the night sky. The bursts of colors and patterns were a mesmerizing sight, and the crowd erupted in cheers with each explosion.

Reorientation:

As the night came to a close, I couldn't help but feel a deep sense of pride and gratitude for my country. The day's celebrations had reminded me of the sacrifices made by our forefathers to secure our freedom. It also reinforced the importance of unity and diversity in our nation.

Independence Day wasn't just a day off from work or school; it was a day to reflect on our shared history and the values that bind us together as a nation. It was a reminder that freedom is a precious gift that we should never take for granted.

32. contoh cerita bahasa inggris tentang bukber bareng teman sertakan orientation, event, reorientation, (beserta terjemahan nya)​


Jawaban:

Orientasi:

Minggu lalu, saya dan teman-teman saya merencanakan untuk mengadakan bukber (berbuka puasa) bersama selama bulan Ramadan. Kami sudah berteman lama dan selalu menantikan momen-momen spesial untuk berkumpul bersama.

Event:

Kami memutuskan untuk mengadakan bukber di sebuah restoran lokal yang menyajikan masakan tradisional Indonesia. Kami memesan meja besar dan meminta restoran untuk menyiapkan beberapa hidangan spesial untuk kami.

Pada hari bukber, kami tiba di restoran beberapa menit sebelum waktu Maghrib. Meja yang kami tempati dihias indah dengan taplak meja berwarna-warni dan dekorasi yang berhubungan dengan Ramadan. Kami semua duduk dan menunggu panggilan adzan.

Setelah adzan berkumandang, kami berbuka puasa dengan kurma dan air. Kemudian, kami menikmati hidangan lezat yang telah disiapkan oleh restoran. Kami makan rendang, sate, nasi goreng, dan banyak hidangan lain yang sangat enak.

Kami menghabiskan berjam-jam untuk berbincang-bincang dan mengetahui kabar satu sama lain. Ini merupakan malam yang indah penuh tawa dan kebahagiaan.

Reorientasi:

Setelah makan malam, kami memutuskan untuk berjalan-jalan di sekitar lingkungan untuk membantu mencerna makanan. Kami berjalan santai dan menikmati suasana Ramadan yang meriah. Kami melihat banyak keluarga yang berbuka puasa bersama-sama, dan kami merasa bersyukur bisa merayakan bulan yang istimewa ini bersama teman-teman.

Saat kami berjalan, kami berbicara tentang rencana kami untuk sisa bulan Ramadan dan perayaan Eid yang akan datang. Kami semua setuju untuk terus mendukung satu sama lain dan memanfaatkan sebaik-baiknya bulan yang diberkahi ini.

Sebagai kesimpulan, bukber bersama teman-teman saya adalah acara yang luar biasa yang membuat kami semakin dekat. Ini menjadi pengingat akan pentingnya menghabiskan waktu bersama orang-orang yang kita sayangi, terutama selama acara-acara spesial seperti Ramadan. Kami pulang dari restoran dengan perasaan bahagia dan puas, dan kami tak sabar untuk melakukannya lagi tahun depan

ENGLISH

Orientation:

Last week, me and my buddies planned to have a bukber (breaking the fast) together during Ramadan. We've been friends for a long time, and we always look forward to spending time together during special occasions.

Event:

We decided to have the bukber at a local restaurant that serves traditional Indonesian food. We made a reservation for a large table and asked the restaurant to prepare some special dishes for us.

On the day of the bukber, we arrived at the restaurant a few minutes before the Maghrib prayer time. The table was beautifully decorated with colorful tablecloths and Ramadan-themed decorations. We all took our seats and waited for the call to prayer.

Once the call to prayer was heard, we broke our fast with dates and water. Then, we enjoyed the delicious food that the restaurant had prepared for us. We had rendang, sate, nasi goreng, and many other dishes that we all enjoyed.

We spent hours chatting and catching up with each other's lives. It was a wonderful evening filled with laughter and joy.

Reorientation:

After the dinner, we decided to take a walk around the neighborhood to help digest the food. We strolled around and enjoyed the festive Ramadan atmosphere. We saw many families breaking their fast together, and we felt grateful to be able to celebrate this special month with our friends.

As we walked, we talked about our plans for the rest of the Ramadan month and our upcoming Eid celebration. We all agreed that we would continue to support each other and make the most out of this blessed month.

In conclusion, the bukber with my friends was a great event that brought us closer together. It was a reminder of the importance of spending time with loved ones, especially during special occasions like Ramadan. We left the restaurant feeling happy and satisfied, and we can't wait to do it again next year.
