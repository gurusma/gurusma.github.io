2. Jawaban bahasa Inggris kelas 11 hal 9 ​


1. How about we go stargazing tonight?

2. How about stay at home and watch TV.

3. Why don't we go to the library?

4. Would you like a cup of coffee?

5. I'll do the washing, if you like?

6. I will help you with half of it if you want.

7. Why don't you go and ask her?

Pembahasan

Di dalam soal di atas kita lihat topik offering help (menawarkan bantuan) yang merupakan salah satu aspek penting dalam bahasa Inggris yang memungkinkan kita untuk menunjukkan kepedulian dan keramahan terhadap orang lain.

1. Bentuk kalimat:

  - Can I help you? (Bisakah saya membantu Anda?)

  - Do you need any assistance? (Apakah Anda membutuhkan bantuan?)

2. Cara menawarkan bantuan:

  - "Let me know if there's anything I can do to help." (Beri tahu saya jika ada yang bisa saya bantu.)

  - "I'd be more than happy to assist you." (Saya dengan senang hati akan membantu Anda.)

3. Respon menerima bantuan:

  - "Thank you, I appreciate it." (Terima kasih, saya menghargainya.)

  - "That would be great, thank you." (Itu akan bagus, terima kasih.)

A: And it's not just about career goals; personal goals are equally important for self-improvement.

B: I couldn't agree more. Setting goals in various aspects of life helps us become a well-rounded individual.

Closing:

A: So, it's essential for us to never stop dreaming and keep setting new goals to pursue.

B: Absolutely! Let's continue to strive for our dreams and make our lives meaningful and fulfilling.

A: Thanks for this inspiring chat. It's always great to discuss these things with someone who shares the same perspective.

B: You're welcome! I feel the same way. It's refreshing to talk about the significance of aspirations in our lives.

A: Alright then, let's keep supporting each other in achieving our goals!

B: Definitely! Best of luck in all your endeavors, and let's stay motivated on our journey!

37. Soal Bahasa Inggris Kelas 11 SMK Tolong di jawabkan Please​


Jawaban:

1. Procedure Text is a text that is designed to describe how something is achieved through a sequence of actions or steps.

2. Generic Structure of Procedure Text:

Goals/ Purpose : It is containt the purpose of the text.Material and tools : it is containt of the materials that used in the process.Steps /methods : it is containt of the steps to make something in the goal.

3. The purpose of procedure text:

To explain/tell (the reader) how to make/operate/do something through a sequence of actions or steps.To explain steps/instruction to make/operate/do something

4. HOW TO MAKE A COFFEE

MATERIALS AND TOOLS :

2 spoons of sugarone spoon of coffee powderhot watera cupa spoon

STEPS :

1. Prepare two spoons of sugar, a cup, hot water, one spoon of coffee powder, a spoon.

2. Put one spoon of coffee powder into the cup.

3. Pour some hot water into the cup.

4. Add 2 spoons of sugar into a cup of coffee

5. Stir it well and the hot coffee is ready to drink.
