14. Sinonim bahasa inggris,hence,energy,suppose,however,happen,tractor,adjust


1. Hence
For this reason
Synonym = Therefore, thus, accordingly, so consequently

2. Energy
The strength and vitality required for sustained physical or mental activity
Synonym = Spirit, liveliness, vibrancy, vitality, life, strength, stamina, power, drive, fire

3. Suppose
Assume that something is the case on the basis of evidence or probability but without proof or certain knowledge
Synonym = Assume, presume, expect, think, believe, suspect, trust

4. However
Used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or seems to contradict something that has been said previously
Synonym = Nevertheless, nonetheless, but, still, yet, though, although

5. Happen
Take place or be experienced by someone
Synonym = Occur, arise, result, betide

6. Tractor
A wheeled vehicle with large wheels, used in farming and other applications
Synonym = Skidder, bulldozer, dozer, truck, vehicle

7. Adjust
Alter or move slightly in order to achieve the desired fit, appearance, or result
Synonym = Modify, alter, arrange, rearrange, remodel, reshape, rework, improve

35. Mau Tanya Soal Bahasa Inggris Perbedaan penggunaan save, saved, saving, dan saves itu apa, terus contoh'a gimana. tolong dibantu ya!


save = menyimpan, saved = Telah tersimpan, dan saves = Akan disimpan . Berdasarkan kata kerja tak beraturan.Kt dasarnya adl save, yg bila verb artinya menyimpan. 1) pd simple present tense, save bila bertemu subject i, you, they, we; saves jika bertemu subject she, he, it. 2) saved adl bentuk past verb atau disebut verb2. 3) saving adl bentuk continuous tense. Biasanya verb+ing diikuti dg to be. 4) saved yg berbentuk past participle atau biasa disebut verb3. Biasanya diikuti dg has, have, had.

37. what are regenaritve breaking system ,and how do they save energy​


Regenerative braking systems are a type of braking system commonly found in electric and hybrid vehicles. These systems use the energy generated during braking to recharge the vehicle's battery.

When a driver applies the brakes in a vehicle with a regenerative braking system, the electric motor that powers the vehicle becomes a generator. This generates electricity, which is then stored in the vehicle's battery. By using this energy to recharge the battery, the vehicle's range is extended, and less energy needs to be consumed from the battery to power the vehicle.

Regenerative braking systems can save energy in several ways. First, they help to extend the life of the vehicle's battery by recharging it during braking. Second, they help to improve the vehicle's fuel efficiency by reducing the amount of energy needed to power the vehicle. Third, regenerative braking systems can also improve vehicle performance by providing instant torque and increased power during acceleration.

Overall, regenerative braking systems are an efficient and effective way to save energy in vehicles. They help to improve fuel efficiency, extend the life of the vehicle's battery, and improve vehicle performance.
