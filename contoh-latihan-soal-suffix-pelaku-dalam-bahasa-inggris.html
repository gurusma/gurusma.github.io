3. Berikan contoh suffix al, less, full masing masing lima per kata dalam bahasa inggris


Suffix -al:
The suffix -al is usually used to change adjectives/verbs into nouns. For example:
1. Proposal (from the word propose)
2. Dismissal (from the word dismiss)
3. Personal (from the word person)
4. Arrival (from the word arrive)
5. Optional (from the word option)

Suffix -less:
The suffix -less represents the absence/unavailability of an adjective/noun. For instance:
1. Helpless (from the word help)
2. Useless (from the word use)
3. Careless (from the word care)
4. Harmless (from the word harm)
5. Senseless (from the word sense)

Suffix -ful:
The suffix -ful is used to change nouns to adjectives, to represent how is a people like/what does he/she feels at the moment. Example:
1. Colorful (from the word color)
2. Harmful (from the word harm)
3. Fearful (from the word fear)
4. Careful (from the word care)
5. Dreadful (from the word dread)

19. Contoh paragraf preffix dan suffix


Suffix merupakan turunan atau imbuhan pada pembentukan pada akhir kata, yang memiliki arti berbeda setelah diturunan. Contoh : Marry menjadi Marriage, kata imbuhan dari ‘ age ‘, artinya pernikahan.

Preffix merupakan turunan atau imbuhan pada pembentukan pada awal kata, yang memiliki arti berbeda setelah diturunan. Contoh : Crime menjadi Cybercrime, kata imbuhan dari ‘ cyber ‘, artinya Kejahatan dengan memanfaatkan IT.

Contoh paragraf preffix:

Correct = Incorrect, artinya tidak benar.

Contoh suffix:

Adore = Adorable, artinya Mengagumkan / menggemaskan.

Contoh kalimat : She makes me so adorable., even I knew she was married.

2. Avoid = Avoidable, artinya Bisa dicegah.

Contoh kalimat : This can be avoidable if he is careful in driving.

Subject : English

Category : Derivatives

Level : SHS

Code : 5

Keywords : prefix, suffix

-----------

Prefix is a letter or letters attached to the beginning of a word and modifies its meaning.

replay = The referee gave Liverpool the penalty after watching the replay of the incident.impossible = It is impossible that pigs can fly.semifinal = The team lost the semifinal.tricycle = My baby brother is riding his tricycle.bipedal = Humans are bipedal. They walk on two legs.

-----------

Suffix is a letter or letters attached to the end of a word and modifies its meaning.

teacher = Mr Smith is an English teacher.sickness = The sickness caused him to lose his hearing.happily = She smiles happily.comfortable = this chair is very comfortable.homeless = After he lost his house he became homeless.

-------------

Words which have both prefix and suffix :

disrespectfully = The rude boys always speak disrespectfully to their mother.anticlockwise = Turn the knob anticlockwise to open the door.uncertainly = He uncertainly answered the question.dissatisfied = Were we dissatisfied with the service offered by the hotel.impossibility = The impossibility of the task despairs him.

33. pengertian suffix dan contohnya


suffix adalah huruf atau kelompok huruf yang ditempatkan di akhir kata atau root (bentuk paling sederhana (base form) dari suatu kata — tidak dapat diuraikan lagi) untuk menghasilkan kata baru.
contoh:
happy (adjective: bahagia) + -ness = happiness (noun: kebahagiaan)
active (adjective: aktif) + -ate = activate (verb: mengaktifkan)
play (verb: bermain) + -ful = playful (adjective: suka bermain)
clear (adjective: jelas) + -ly = clearly (adverb: dengan jelas)
