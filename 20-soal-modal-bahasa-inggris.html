A few hours later, we were arrived in the Borobudur Temple area. Then, we buy 4 tickets. One for me, one for my Mom one for my Dad, and one for my little sister. After that, we were enter and see how beautiful borobur temple. We were very happy. We were take some photos there. And so many local tourists and foreign tourists too. Then, I was ask one of foreign tourist to take a photo with my family and I. Well, they were so friendly. And they were looks so happy. We were buy some souvenirs, like miniature of Borobudur Temple, some T-shirts, etc. After we were feel tired, then we were find a hotel then went back tomorrow.

It was my best experience. Someday maybe we will went here again.
