15. Buatlah contoh dialog tentang handling n making complain dalam bahasa inggris. Maksimal 2 orang !


Answer: 

In making complaint, especially in speaking, one tends to use offensive and informal words since one is normally carried away by anger, whereas in writing, such word is not used at all. One still has to use formal words and sentences to express the complaint clearly but in a less emotional way. Therefore, euphemism is included. Meanwhile, in handling complaints, one always has to use proper formal sentence to respect the ones who file the complaint. Here's the example of such expression: 
- I'm sorry for the inconvenience.
- My apology for the mistakes. We will change it immediately. 

Here's the dialogue:
O: Excuse me, Miss, please come here.
W: Yes, Ma'am, how may I help you? 
O: This steak isn't well-done. I ordered well-done, Miss, not to mention that this is not a fish steak but chicken one. 
W: Oh, is it? My apology for that, Ma'am. I'll have it changed immediately.
O: Hold on, not so fast. I demand a discount and get free desert. 
W: Hmm, I'm sorry, Miss, I'm afraid I am in not such position to authorize such service. 
O: OK, can I see your manager then? This is preposterous. 
W: I'm sorry but our manager happens to be hospitalised today. 
O: Oh, hmm, OK, then, get me the right order. Be quick about it.
W: Certainly, Ma'am, hold a minute. 
     (this lady can do nothing but whining) 
(At the kitchen)
W: Can you get me a security guard, handle that lady. 
S: Yes, Ms. manager. We'll take care of it.

Mapel: English
Kategori: complaint

23. Apa ada yang tau alasan dari contoh passive voice seperti ini : "batik" making is traditionally made by women either at home ar in a small workshop.. tolong kasih tau dengan jawaban bahasa inggris dan bahasa indonesia


The reason of above passive voice is the using of phrasal subject itself. Phrasal subject is the subject in form of "phrase" or subject that consist of more than one words example "Batik making", if we translate the phrase the meaning will be "the process to make batik". Then the rest of the sentence are grammatically correct.

alasan pasif di atas adalah ia menggunakan subjek dalam bentuk frasa. Subjek dalam bentuk frasa adalah subjek yang terdiri dari dua kata contohnya Making batik jika di terjemahkan artinya "prosess pembuatan batik" Seterusnya kalimat setelah itu kalimatnya udah benar dilihat dari tata bahasa Inggris.

25. contoh dialog making request, making sugestion making complaints, giving instructions


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: Bhs Inggris
Kategori: expression
Kata kunci: request, suggestion, complaint, instruction

1. Making request
J: Sweetie, can you fetch me those chopped onions?
S: Okay, mom. Anyway, would you mind if I go out of town with Carl this weekend?
J: Well, I don't think it's a good idea if only two of you, bring your friend too.
S: But, mom......
J: Sorry, young lady, you're not allowed, unless you're with someone else too.

2.Suggestion
C: Dude, what shall we do this weekend?
D: Shall we play badminton?
C: Not again, man, I'd rather do something else.
D: Oh, okay. Hey, I just remember that there's a big match this weekend, Arsenal vs Liverpool, why don't we watch it live?
C: At the stadium? That'll be awesome.
D: OK, let's get the ticket then.

3.Complaints
O: Hello, how may I help you, Ma'am?
H: Yes, Miss, you can actually. I'd like to file a complaint about one of your porters here. He's been making me uncomfortable during my stay. His name is Patrice. He has been improperly asking me far too many personal questions.
O: Oh, Patrice Lacombe. I shall have him warned then. My apology for that, Ma'am.

4.Instruction
P: Excuse me, could you help me out, please?
W: Yes, how can I help?
P: I'd like to download videos from youtube via Firefox browser. How can you do that?
W: OK, I'll tell you what to so. Follow my lead, okay?
P: Sure.
W: So, first, click the icon on the top right under the 'close' button. Click the 'add-on' and type 'youtube downloader' on search. You'll have many options for that. Just choose one and install it.
P: OK, got it, is that all?
W: Almost, just close the Firefox and open it again. It'll work.
P: Thank you then.

Pembahasan

Apa itu promises?

Promises adalah sebuah komitmen oleh seseorang untuk melakukan atau untuk tidak melakukan sesuatu atau ungkapan bahwa suatu hal akan akan atau tidakan akan dilakukan atau diberikan.

Making promise adalah ekspresi tentang bagaimana mengekspresikan janjimu kepada seseorang.  

Contoh kalimat dalam making promise:

- I promise that i will finish my job

- I promise you that that’s the truth

- I swear I won’t let you down

- I assure you that I will return the book

Contoh respon yang dapat digunakan :

- Thank you

- I believe you

- All right. Keep your promise

- I hope you will keep your promise
