9. contoh Procedure text about how to make cheese Banana dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Goal : To Make a Cheese Banana
Tujuan : Untuk membuat Pisang Keju

Ingredients / Materials ( Bahan - bahan )
6 medium-sized bananas, peeled
( 6 pisang berukuran sedang yang sudah dikupas )
3 tbsp rice flour
( 3 sendok teh tepung beras )
3 tbsp wheat flour
( 3 sendok teh tepung gandum )
1/2 tsp vanilla essence
( 1/2 sendok teh perasa vanilla )
2 tbsp milk creamer
( 2 sendok teh krimer susu )
Grated cheese
( Keju yang sudah diparut )
Oil for deep-frying
( Minyak untuk menggoreng )
A pinch of salt
( Sejumput garam )
A pinch of sugar
( Sejumput gula )

Steps ( Langkah - langkah ) :
1.First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water.
( Pertama, campur gula, tepung beras dan tepung gandum dengan air )
2.Then soak bananas in the batter. Heat the oil in a large wok.
( Kemudian, rendam pisang dalam mentega. Panaskan api di wajan yang besar )
3.Then drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas.
( Kemudian, masukkan satu persatu pisang ke dalam minyak. Masak cukup dua pisang saja dalam satu waktu karena kalau ada terlalu banyak pisang, suhu minyaknya akan menurun drastis )
4.When bananas are crisp and golden brown, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel and put aside.
( Ketika pisangnya sudah garing dan berwarna cokelat keemasan, angkat dari minyak, keringkan di tisu dan letakkan di tempat lain )
5.Pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas.
( Tuang krimer susu di atas pisang yang sudah masak )
6.Finally, coat it with cheese.
( Terakhir, lapisi pisang itu dengan keju )

11. How to make fried rice ->> procedure text buat lah dalam bahasa inggris!!


Jawaban:

How to Make Fried Rice

Ingredients:

- 1 plate of cooked rice

- 2 eggs

- 1 teaspoon of salt

- 2 medium onions, chopped

- 1 cloves of garlic, chopped

- Chili powder (adjust based on your spicy level)

- 1 tablespoon of tomato sauce

- 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Utensils:

- Frying pan

- Stove

- Spoon

- Spatula

- Plate

Steps of how to cook fried rice:

1. Heat a medium frying pan and pour the vegetable oil.

2. After it’s heated enough, add the garlic, onion, and chili.

3. Stir-fry the ingredients until it’s tender.

4. Add the eggs and scramble it until half cooked.

5. Pour the cooked rice, then keep stir for a minute.

6. Next, add the seasoning such as the sauce, pepper, and salt, and mix them all with the rice.

7. After it’s well cooked, take the fried rice and serve on the plate.

- Penggorengan

How to Make Chocolate pudding
Material :
- Agar-Agar White 1 Pack
- Milk 400 Ml Liquid
- 50 gr Dark Cooking Chocolate, Chopped
- 2 Tablespoons Granulated Sugar
- 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
- Egg Whites 3 Eggs 

Tools:Pan, Bowl, Stove, Spoon, Mold, Refrigerator, Water
Steps:

First, Agar-agar cook with milk, chocolate and sugar over medium heat until hot.
Second, take a little dough, then mix with the egg yolks, pour back into the hot mixture. Cook until boiling. Lift.
Next, Beaten egg whites until fluffy and up. Then pour the hot gelatin mixture gradually while stirring constantly until well blended. 
Then, Save or pour into a mold. Allow it to freeze, so quickly refrigerate until cold. 
Finally, prepared pudding served with a complement.

How to Make Brownies 


Ingredients
• 150gram of butter.
• 150gram of dark chocolate.
• 300 gram of flour                 
• 4 eggs.
• 600 gram of regular sugar
• 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
1. Melt the chocolate with the butter.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla extract.
3. Preheat the oven at low temperature
4. Combine the mixes you made.
5. Add the flour and stir with a wooden spoon.
