13. Buatkan dialog the hot water in the shower isn' working properly


(on the phone)

(Di telepon)

A: Hello, good morning!

Halo. Selamat pagi!

B: Hello, this is HS Service. What can I do for you?

Halo. Ini Servis HS. Ada yang bisa saya bantu?

A: Err... I'm Richard. You know, the hot water in the shower isn't working properly. I don't know what to do. I have tried to fix it many times but it's still not working.

Err. Ini Richard. Kamu tahu, air panas di kamar mandi tidak bekerja dengan baik. Saya tidak tahu apa yang harus saya lakukan. Saya sudah memperbaikinya berulang-ulang namun masih tidak bisa bekerja.

B: Have you checked the heating elements? Maybe there is malfunction.

Sudahkah anda mengecek elemen pemanas? Mungkin ada yang rusak.

A: I have it checked. But I think it's fine. Could you come and help me to repair it?

Saya sudah periksa. Namun saya kira baik-baik saja. Bisakah anda datang dan membantu saya memperbaikinya?

B: Sure Mr. Richard. I will send a repairman to come to your house. Can you tell me your address?

Tentu Pak Richard. Saya akan mengirim tukang ke rumah anda. Bisakah anda beritahu alamat anda?

A: Yes. I live in Jalan Pajajaran, No. 7. Surabaya

Ya saya tinggal di Jalan Pajajaran, No. 7. Surabaya

B: Okay, noted.

OK, dicatat

A: OK. Thanks a lot.

OK. Terima kasih banyak.

Pembahasan

The above dialog shows the example of offering and asking for service expressions.  The phrase "What can I do for you?" indicates offering service and the phrase "Could you come and help me to repair it?" indicates asking for service.

Below are the other expressions of offering help :

What can I do for you?

The responses are :

Sure, thanks

Yes, please. Thank you

Below are the expressions of asking for help:

Can you help me?

Could you help me please?

Would you mind helping please?

The responses are:

No problem

Sure

I'm sorry, I can't do it

No. I'm really busy now.

34. Don' t worry . The river isn' t ... it looksA. the deepest B. more deep than C. as deep as D. deep ​


Jawaban:

Kalimat dalam soal:

Don't worry. The river isn't ____ it looks. (Jangan khawatir. Sungai itu tidak ____ terlihat.)

Opsi jawaban yang bisa kita pilih:

A. deep

B. as deep as

C. more deep than

D. the deepest

Berarti konteks kalimat pada soal ini adalah membandingkan 2 objek yang setara yaitu, sungai yang disebutkan tidak sedalam dengan apa yang terlihat.

Maka, konteks kata sifat (adjective) yang digunakan adalah "Positive Adjective".

Kata adjective "deep"

As + Deep (Adjective) + As

Jadi, opsi jawaban yang tepat untuk soal ini adalah opsi B. As Deep As (sedalam).

Don't worry. The river isn't as deep as it looks.

(Jangan khawatir. Sungai itu tidak sedalam yang terlihat.)
