I woke up at 5 am. Then I got up to take Subuh prayer. Afterward I tidied up my bathroom such as opening the window, turning off the lamp, sweeping the floor and arranging my pillow and bolsters and folding my blanket.
After that I went outside my house to work out. I did jogging around my house then I pushed up and sit up to build my muscle. After doing sport, I took a rest for a while. Then I took bath on my bathroom and brushed my teeth. Next I got dress my school uniform and looked to the mirror. I took my comb then I combed my hair.

While I prepared to go to school, my mother made me a breakfast in the kitchen. Then my mother called me to have a breakfast and then I went out from my bedroom to have a breakfast. I saw my father reading a newspaper in the living room so I asked my father to have a breakfast together. We had a breakfast at 7.30 am. After having breakfast, my mother gave me a glass of milk so I drank the milk. Afterwards, I helped my mother to clean the dishes. Next I used my shoes and prepared my motorcycle from the garage. Before I went to school, I asked permission to my mother and my father and shook their hands. Then I turned on my motorcycle and went to school.

15. buatlah daily activity dalam bahasa indonesia dan inggris​


daily activity bhs.inggris

The alarm clock goes off at 5.30 a.m. I wake up in the morning then drink 2 glasses of water. I feel very fresh after drinking water. I wash my face so I do not feel sleepy. I do some physical exercises. I keep my body fit and healthy. Also, I keep my room neat and clean.

I have prepared my books at previous night. In the morning, I need to check books in my bag. After that, I prepare myself to school, have breakfast at 6.45 in the morning. I go to school at 7 o’clock. My school is far from my house. My father rides me to school and pick me up at 1 p.m.

After school, I help mother to sell goods in our shop. I also study there. At 6 o’clock I go home, take a bath and pray. After that, I play computer games for one hour then review some lessons. Finally, at 10 p.m. I go to sleep.

Enrich Your Vocabulary!

feel very fresh = merasa sangat segar

I do not feel sleepy = saya tidak merasa ngantuk

keep my body fit and healthy = menjaga tubuh saya tetep kuat dan sehat

at previous night = malam sebelumnya alias kemarin malam

check books in my bad = memeriksa buku-buku di dalam tas saya

far from my house = jauh dari rumah saya

ride me to school and pick me up = mengantar ke sekolah dan menjemput saya

sell goods in our shop = menjual barang-barang di toko kami

review some lessons = mengulang beberapa pelajaran

terjemahan bhs indonesia

Jam alarm berbunyi jam 5.30 pagi. Saya bangun di pagi hari kemudian minum 2 gelas air. Saya merasa sangat segar setelah minum air. Saya mencuci muka jadi saya tidak merasa mengantuk. Saya melakukan beberapa latihan fisik. Saya menjaga tubuh saya tetap bugar dan sehat. Juga, saya menjaga kamar saya tetap rapi dan bersih.

Saya sudah menyiapkan buku-buku saya pada malam sebelumnya. Di pagi hari, saya perlu memeriksa buku di tas saya. Setelah itu, saya mempersiapkan diri ke sekolah, sarapan pukul 6.45 pagi. Saya pergi ke sekolah pada jam 7. Sekolah saya jauh dari rumah saya. Ayah saya mengantarku ke sekolah dan menjemputku jam 1 malam.

Sepulang sekolah, saya membantu ibu menjual barang di toko kami. Saya juga belajar di sana. Jam 6 sore saya pulang, mandi dan berdoa. Setelah itu, saya bermain game komputer selama satu jam kemudian meninjau beberapa pelajaran. Akhirnya, pada jam 10 malam Aku pergi tidur.

27. write a descriptive text entitled My Daily Routines !​


Jawaban:

My daily routine is a well-oiled machine that keeps me organized and on track. I begin my day early, usually around 6:00 am, with a refreshing cup of coffee and a quick breakfast. I then take some time to plan out my day, making a list of the tasks and errands that need to be completed.

After my morning routine, I start my exercise. I usually go for a run or a bike ride, which helps me to wake up and feel energized for the day ahead. I also do yoga or stretching to keep my body flexible and healthy.

Once I am back home, I take a shower and get ready for work. I start my workday at 9:00 am and spend the next several hours working on projects and meetings. I take a lunch break at noon, where I usually make a healthy meal at home or grab something quick on the go.

After work, I like to spend some time outdoors, either going for a walk or just enjoying the fresh air. I also use this time to catch up with friends and family. I also have a night routine where I read, journal and meditate before I go to bed.

Overall, my daily routine is a balance of work, exercise, and relaxation. It helps me to stay organized, productive, and healthy, both physically and mentally.

39. buat aktifitas daily dalam bahasa inggris


MY ACTIVITY

I usually wake up at 5 o’clock in the morning. I never forget to tidy up my bed and clean it up. Then I take a bath and pray. After that I help my mother to prepare the breakfast. And then, I have breakfast with my family in the dining room. We usually have warm tea, milk and fried rice. Then, I go to school with my friends. I study at school till 1 p.m. I go home, put off my uniform and have lunch with my mother. After that I pray, watch TV and take a nap. I always take a bath at 5 p.m. in the afternoon. Then I watch TV while waiting the time for praying. After I pray, I and my family have lunch together. And then, I read a book and prepare the lesson for the next school day. I go to bed at 9 p.m. That’s all my activity starting from  waking up till going to be. Thank you.

Terjemahan:
Saya biasanya bangun pukul 5 pagi. Saya tidak pernah lupa untuk merapikan tempat tidur sayadan membersihkannya. Kemudian sayamengambil mandi dan berdoa. Setelah itu sayamembantu ibu saya untuk sarapan. Dankemudian, saya memiliki sarapan dengankeluarga saya di ruang makan. Kami biasanyamemiliki teh hangat, susu dan nasi goreng.Kemudian, aku pergi ke sekolah dengan teman-teman saya. Saya belajar di sekolah sampaipukul 13: 00 Saya pulang ke rumah, menundaseragam saya dan memiliki makan siang denganibu saya. Setelah itu saya berdoa, menonton TVdan beristirahat. Aku selalu mengambil mandipukul 5 sore. Kemudian saya menonton TVsambil menunggu waktu untuk berdoa. Setelahsaya berdoa, saya dan keluarga saya memilikimakan siang bersama. Dan kemudian, sayamembaca buku dan mempersiapkan pelajaransekolah keesokan harinya. Aku pergi tidur di 9Itulah semua aktivitas saya mulai bangun sampai akan menjadi. Terima kasih.
