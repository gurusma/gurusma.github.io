18. contoh soal bahasa inggris dan jawabannya kelas 10 semester 2 soal HOTS​


Berikut ini adalah beberapa contoh soal Bahasa Inggris tingkat kelas 10 semester 2 dengan tingkat kesulitan tinggi (HOTS) beserta jawabannya:

Soal 1:

Read the following passage and answer the questions that follow.

Passage:

Climate change is a pressing global issue that requires immediate action. It is causing a rise in global temperatures, resulting in melting ice caps, sea-level rise, and extreme weather events. The consequences of climate change are far-reaching and impact both human and natural systems. To mitigate the effects of climate change, it is crucial to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, promote renewable energy sources, and implement sustainable practices.

Questions:

1. What are the consequences of climate change?

2. What measures can be taken to mitigate the effects of climate change?

Jawaban:

1. The consequences of climate change include melting ice caps, sea-level rise, and extreme weather events.

2. Measures that can be taken to mitigate the effects of climate change include reducing greenhouse gas emissions, promoting renewable energy sources, and implementing sustainable practices.

Soal 2:

Choose the correct word to complete the sentence.

1. The teacher asked the students to ________ their assignments by tomorrow.

a) submit

b) submitted

c) submitting

d) submits

Jawaban: a) submit

Soal 3:

Rearrange the following words to form a meaningful sentence.

easy / It / is / to / English / learn.

Jawaban: It is easy to learn English.

Soal 4:

Read the dialogue and answer the question that follows.

Tom: Have you ever been to Paris?

Sara: No, I haven't. But I would love to visit someday.

Question:

What is Sara's response to Tom's question?

Jawaban: Sara's response to Tom's question is, "No, I haven't. But I would love to visit someday."

Semoga contoh soal di atas dapat membantu kamu dalam mempersiapkan diri untuk menghadapi soal Bahasa Inggris kelas 10 semester 2 yang menguji kemampuan berpikir tingkat tinggi (HOTS).

28. temukan arti kata yang berhubungan dengan silsilah keluarga dalam bahasa inggris​


Halo kak! kata yang berhubungan dengan silsilah keluarga dalam bahasa Inggris adalah "genealogy" atau "family tree."

Penjelasan:

1. Genealogy: Genealogy refers to the study or investigation of the history and lineage of families, typically through the tracing of their ancestors and descendants. It involves collecting and organizing information about family members, their relationships, and their ancestral origins.

2. Family Tree: A family tree is a graphical representation of a person's ancestors and descendants. It typically starts with an individual at the base and branches out to include parents, grandparents, great-grandparents, and so on. Each individual on the tree is connected by lines to show their familial relationships.

Both terms are often used interchangeably when discussing the exploration of one's family history and connections. Genealogy is the broader concept, encompassing the research and documentation of family history, while a family tree is a visual representation of that history.

31. 1. Mencari makna penting keluarga Dan urutan silsilah keluarga​


Keluarga adalah penggenapan janji Tuhan. Tempat dimana kami mempraktekkan anugerah Tuhan kepada satu sama lain, tempat untuk bertumbuh, mengasihi, menerima perbedaan, kekurangan, dan saling mengampuni. Orang yang kamu nyaman berada disekitar mereka dan bisa menjadi diri sendiri. Hubungan dimana waktu tidak berpengaruh.

Urutan silsilah keluarga dalam bahasa Indonesia adalah cilawagi, buyut, moyang, nenek, ibu/bapak, ego, anak, cucu, cicit, piut, dan anggas.
