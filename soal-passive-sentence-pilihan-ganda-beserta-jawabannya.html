Active : kalau diartikan kata kerjanya (verb-nya) menggunakan imbuhan "me-", biasanya ditandai dengan Subject yang mengerjakan aktivitas. Bentuk kalimatnya biasanya :

Subject + to be / Verb + Object

Dan ciri-ciri lainnya adalah bentuk kalimat biasanya seperti ini :

Object + to be (is/am/are/was/were) + V3 + by Subject

1. Somebody cleans the room every day.
Passive voice is:

(A) The room was clean by somebody every day.
(B) The room is cleaned by somebody every day.
(C) The room is clean by somebody every day.
(D) Somebody is cleaned the room every day.
2. Sam has taken an English course.
Passive voice is:

(A) Sam has been taken an English course.
(B) An English course taken by Sam.
(C) An English course has been taken by Sam.
(D) English courses have taken by Sam.
3. The students are using the computers now.
Passive voice is:

(A) The computers are being used by the students now.
(B) The computers are used by them now.
(C) The computers were used by them now.
(D) The computers is being used by them now.
4. My father fixed the car yesterday.
Passive voice is:

(A) My father was fixed the car yesterday.
(B) My father was being fixed the car yesterday.
(C) The car was fix by my father yesterday.
(D) The car was fixed by my father yesterday.
5. The kid has broken the window.
Passive voice:

(A) The window has been broken by the kid.
(B) The window will be broken by the kid.
(C) The window is being broken by the kid.
(D) The window is broken by the kid.

16. buatlah 10 soal tentang passive voice. beserta jawabannya​


Passive voice adalah bentuk kalimat pasif yang lebih mengutamakan obyek atau prosesnya ketimbang pelakunya. Dalam membuat soal, kita harus menguasai terlebih dahulu konsep dari passive voice. Pola sederhananya adalah

O + modals / auxiliary verb + V3 + complement / by + S

O adalah obyek dan S adalah subyek. Modals / auxiliary verb bisa bervariasi bergantung pada jenis tense yang digunakan, apakah itu past, present atau future.

Penjelasan:

Untuk jenis soal ada berbagai macam model yang dipilih, mulai dari gapfill, multiple choice atau sentence writing.

Berikut ini adalah contoh soal model pertama

'The Greyfield Stadium was one of the most famous stadium here back in the 1950s. It .......(build) in 1949 and ......(own) by FC Soton United. Unfortunately, it .......(not equip) with the standard electricity and security system. No wonder that the stadium......(burgle) several times in 1961-1962.

Currently, this ......(pull down) due to the fact that it .....(not consider) suitable based on the latest stadium standard. Now, it ......(rebuild) as the training centre for Southampton since the previous owner, FC Soton United, .....(dismiss) in 2005 because of its poor financial management.

The Greyfield, as it .....(call)now, in fact, ....... (locate) nearby St.Mary stadium, the homebase of FC Southampton.

jawaban:

was built (past passive as the time signal is obvious)was owned (same reason as above)was not equipped (same reason as above)had been burgled (past perfect as it is followed by the number of event happened at the certain period in the past)has been pulled down (present perfect as there is a word 'currently', suits for that situation)is not considered (present passive as the context is now)is being rebuilt (present continous passive because it's about activity now)was dismissed (same reason as no 1-3)is called (present passive, which is about situation now)is located (same reason as above)

adalah kalimat tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau dan telah berakhir di masa lampau.

Untuk membentuk kalimat simple past tense, rumusnya adalah sebagai berikut

S + Verb 2 + O

Dalam kalimat simple past tense, kata kerja / verb yang digunakan merupakan bentuk kata kerja  kedua. Terdapat 2 jenis kata kerja / Verb, yakni Regular Verb dan Irregular Verb. Untuk regular verb, tambahkan -ed / -d dibelakang setelah kata kerja bentuk pertama. Sebagai contoh

Stay -> stayed (Tinggal)Punch -> Punched (Memukul)Play -> Played (Bermain)Touch -> Touched (Menyentuh)

Untuk Irregular verb , termasuk didalamnya to be, bentuk kata kerja keduanya sangat berbeda. Sebagai contoh :

Awake -> Awoke (Terbangun)Begin -> Began (Memulai)Drink -> Drank (Minum)Eat -> ate (Makan)Run -> ran (Berlari)

Namun, ada sebagian Irregular verb yang memiliki bentuk kata kerja yang sama dengan bentuk kata kerja dasar. Sebagai contoh:

Put -> Put (Meletakkan)Split -> Split (Membagi)Spread -> Spread (Menyebarkan)Set -> Set (Mengatur)Cut -> Cut (Memotong)

32. pengertian passive sentence


sebuah kata kerja yang tidak menunjukan aksipassive sentence is the subject does not perform the action in the sentence. In fact, the action is performed on it. 

passive sentence sama sepertinya dengan bahasa Indonesia, yaitu kalimat pasif, dimana objek melakukan tindakan.

pada bahasa Inggris, bentuk passive mengikuti tense active, dengan perubahan verb bentuk ketiga. Ingat, hanya kalimat verbal saja yang bisa diubah menjadi passive sentence.

contoh:
active present: he writes a diary every morning.
passive present : a diary IS WRITTEN by him every morning.

active past : he wrote a diary yesterday
passive past : a diary WAS WRITTEN by him yesterday
