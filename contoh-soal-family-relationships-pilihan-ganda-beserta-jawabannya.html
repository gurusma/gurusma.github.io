18. In our extended family there are many family relationships. Give the definition of COUSIN and NIECE.​


As nouns the difference between cousin and niece

is that cousin is the son or daughter of a person's uncle or aunt; a first cousin while niece is a daughter of someone's sibling, brother-in-law, or sister-in-law; either the daughter of one's brother ("fraternal niece"), or of one's sister ("sororal niece").
