Hi. Here I will tell you about a vacation to the beach of Plabuhan Ratu, Who am I on vacation with? Of course with beloved friends.

before the holidays arrive, my friend and I have talked about the holidays at the end of this year from a long time ago, when the holidays have arrived, we are also ready to welcome the arrival of the holiday with an excursion to Plabuhan Ratu Beach.

With this holiday. We can have fun, hang out with friends, relieve fatigue in our heads. Spend vacation time with this friend by visiting several tourist attractions such as beaches, mountains, hills or many other tourist attractions.

On this holiday, it is very appropriate to visit Plabuhan Ratu Beach. With this very favorable weather atmosphere, we can relax by the beach while enjoying the gentle breeze.

Jawaban:

Vacation with Family to Pelabuhan Ratu

Palabuhanratu or what we usually call Pelabuhan Ratu is one of the favorite tourist destinations in Sukabumi Regency - West Java. Some time ago my extended family and I took a vacation to Pelabuhan Ratu, this is a tourist attraction that offers a variety of natural beauty, from the charm of its beaches, scuba diving spots, hot springs, to exotic bat caves.

We took a long journey from Bandung to Sukabumi. Alhamdulillah, the trip was quite smooth even though the weather was a bit cloudy and it rained. The distance from Bandung to Sukabumi (Pelabuhan Ratu) is about 150 KM.

First we arrived at Citepus beach, the beach is very crowded with visitors, the waves are fairly calm. There we broke for lunch on the beach while laying out mats. The family brings a Sundanese lead menu, the menus include; Timbel rice (wrapped in banana leaves), fried chicken, tofu, tempeh, salted fish, fresh vegetables, chili sauce, and crackers.

Botram lunch

Citepus Beach is the first spot for the gathering point, we are waiting for several family cars that are still on their way from different cities. Here the beach is okay for the main air, the waves are not too big, which is very unfortunate for many visitors who litter.

After waiting for a while, finally everyone gathered and we continued to the next point while looking for hotels and inns in areas close to the beach. In the afternoon, we had time to visit Karang Hawu beach and relax there enjoying the sun slowly starting to set.

Finally, before sunset we get lodging in the Cisolok area near the Cibangban beach. This beach is not as busy as Citepus beach. Cibangban Beach has a panoramic view that spoils the eye, beautiful surrounding scenery, the beach is quiet, it's like a private beach. We may all be 40 people from 8 families. Spend the night near the waterfront.

Me and my big family

The warm inn is very warm. Most of the rooms are predominantly family. At night we smoke fireworks and gather together while enjoying an exciting family vacation. We have found a beautiful sunset and definitely hunting for the sunrise from Cibangban beach.

While heading for the sunrise the children played and ran on the beach

Sunny mornings after the sun started to rise, we played all kinds of games from building sand castles (sand castles) to ladies' beach soccer games. All support each other and are happy.

artinya :

Liburan Bersama Keluarga ke Pelabuhan Ratu

Palabuhanratu atau yang biasa kita sebut dengan Pelabuhan Ratu adalah salah satu destinasi wisata favorit di Kabupaten Sukabumi - Jawa Barat. Beberapa waktu lalu saya dan keluarga besar berlibur ke Pelabuhan Ratu, ini adalah salah satu objek wisata yang menawarkan berbagai keindahan alam mulai pesona pantainya, spot scuba diving, pemandian air panas, hingga goa kelelawar yang eksotis.

Perjalanan panjang kami tempuh dari Bandung sampai ke Sukabumi. Alhamdulillah perjalanan terbilang cukup lancar walaupun cuaca memang agak mendung dan sempat hujan. Jarak Bandung - Sukabumi (Pelabuhan Ratu) sekitar 150 KM.

Pertama kami tiba di pantai Citepus, pantainya sangat ramai pengunjung, ombaknya terbilang tenang. Di sana kami istirahat makan siang di pinggir pantai sambil menggelar tikar. Keluarga membawa menu timbel khas Sunda, menunya antara lain; nasi yang ditimbel (dibungkus daun pisang), ayam goreng, tahu, tempe, ikan asin, lalapan, sambal, dan juga kerupuk.

Botram makan siang

Pantai Citepus ini jadi spot pertama untuk titik kumpul, kita menunggu beberapa mobil keluarga yang masih dalam perjalanan dari kota berbeda. Di sini pantainya oke untuk udara utama, ombaknya tidak terlalu besar, yang sangat disayangkan pengunjung banyak yang membuang sampah sembarangan.

Setelah menunggu beberapa lama, akhirnya semua berkumpul dan kita pun berlanjut ke titik selanjutnya sambil mencari hotel dan penginapan di daerah yang dekat dengan pantai. Semakin sore, kita sempat mengunjungi pantai Karang Hawu dan bersantai di sana menikmati matahari yang perlahan mulai tenggelam.

Akhirnya menjelang magrib kita dapat penginapan di daerah Cisolok dekat pantai Cibangban. Pantai ini tidak seramai pantai Citepus. Pantai Cibangban memiliki panorama yang memanjakan mata, pemandangan sekelilingnya yang indah, pantainya sepi, sudah seperti pantai pribadi. Kita semua mungkin 40 orang dari 8 keluarga. Menghabiskan malam di dekat tepi pantai.

Saya dan keluarga besar

Di penginapan hangat sangat hangat. Sebagian besar kamar didominasi keluarga. Pada saat malam kita merokok kembang api dan berkumpul bersama sambil menikmati liburan keluarga yang seru. Kita sudah menemukan matahari terbenam yang indah dan pastinya memburu matahari terbit dari pantai Cibangban.

Sambil menuju matahari terbit anak-anak bermain dan berlarian di tepi pantai

Pagi hari yang cerah setelah matahari mulai naik, kami mengadakan berbagai macam permainan mulai dari membangun istana pasir (istana pasir) sampai permainan sepak bola pantai ibu-ibu. Semua saling support dan bergembira.

Zayn Malik is best known as one of the founding members of the chart-topping boy-band-of-a-generation One Direction. Growing up as a mixed race Pakistani-English Muslim in a post-industrial British town presented some difficulties, which resulted in a number of primary school changes. In March 2015, while the band was on the On the Road Again Tour, Malik abruptly parted ways with One Direction a week after his final appearance with the group in Hong Kong. Citing stress and a desire for a simpler life out of the spotlight, Malik later revealed that he simply wanted to make music that he would listen to and enjoy. Striking out on his own, he signed with RCA Records and moved to Los Angeles, where he began working on his solo effort with James "Malay" Ho (Frank Ocean, Big Boi). His debut single, "Pillowtalk," was released in late January 2016 under the name ZAYN and featured his strong vocals over distinctly more adult-themed alt-R&B similar to The Weeknd and Miguel. 39. Suruh bikin dialog ttg survival pake bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

Person A: Hi there! Have you ever been on a survival adventure before?

Person B: Hi! Yes, I have. I went on a camping trip once, and it turned into a survival situation when we got lost in the wilderness.

Person A: Oh, that sounds intense! How did you manage to survive?

Person B: It was definitely challenging, but we tried to stay calm and collected. First, we built a shelter using branches and leaves to protect ourselves from the elements.

Person A: That's smart! What about food and water?

Person B: Finding water was our top priority. We were lucky to spot a small stream nearby, so we boiled the water to make it safe for drinking. As for food, we gathered wild berries and edible plants, and we even managed to catch a few small fish from the stream.

Person A: Impressive! I don't know if I could do that. How did you handle starting a fire?

Person B: Starting a fire was tricky without matches, but we used a fire starter and dried leaves to create sparks. It took some patience, but eventually, we had a fire going for warmth and cooking.

Person A: Wow, you were well-prepared! What about navigation? How did you find your way back?

Person B: Unfortunately, we didn't have a map or compass, but we knew the general direction of our starting point. During the day, we followed the sun, and at night, we used the stars as our guide.

Person A: That sounds like a real survival adventure! Were there any scary moments?

Person B: Definitely! At one point, we encountered a wild animal, but we made loud noises and scared it away. Also, dealing with the cold nights and constant fear of the unknown was quite scary.

Person A: I can only imagine. How long were you out there before you were rescued?

Person B: We were lost for three days before a search party found us. Those were the longest three days of my life, but the experience taught me a lot about survival and staying positive in tough situations.

Person A: I can imagine it was a life-changing experience. I don't know if I could handle something like that.

Person B: You never know until you're faced with it. It's crucial to stay prepared, stay calm, and rely on your instincts when survival is at stake.

Person A: You're right. It's definitely a good idea to learn some survival skills, just in case. Thanks for sharing your survival story!

Person B: You're welcome! Hopefully, you'll never have to experience something like that, but it's always better to be prepared. Stay safe out there!
