Pembahasan => Asking Ability

Asking Ability adalah Ungkapan yg ditanyakan pada seseorang mengenai kemampuan atau kesanggupan (bisa/mampu).

Can you ... ?Are you good at ... ?Are you able to ... ?Do you know how to ... ?

=> Responding Ability

Responding Ability adalah respon yg menyatakan bisa/mampu. Respon ini diberikan saat ditanya Mengenai kemampuan atau saat memberikan respon bahwa bisa/mampu mengenai sesuatu.

I canI know how to ...I am able toI feel capable to ...

=> Responding Disablility

Responding Disability adalah respon ketidakmampuan. Respon ini diberikan saat merasa tidak bisa atau tidak mampu melakukannya.

I can’t/ I cannot I am not good atI am not sure that I canI am hopeless

Maka percakapan:

A: Look there! there are many birds. Can they fly in the air?

B: Yes, They can fly in the air

A: Why?

B: Birds can fly because they have wings

Penjelasan:

Percakapan terjadi antara Si A dan Si BSi A menanyakan Apakah Burung BISA terbang di udara?Si B menjawab BISA Si A bertanya lagi kenapaSi B memberitahukan alasannya yaitu karena mereka [burung] memiliki sayap.

Kesimpulan:

Can they [birds] fly? (Asking Ability, menanyakan kemampuan burung bisa terbang)Birds can fly because they have wings (Responding Ability, yg diberikan bahwa burung mampu/bisa terbang karena punya sayap)

A: Can we fly like them?

B: No, We can't fly like them, because we don't have wings. But we can fly very high by a plane.

A: Oh I see. Thank you B

B: You’re welcome

Penjelasan:

si A juga penasaran apakah manusia bisa terbang seperti burungsi B menjelaskan bahwa manusia TIDAK BISA terbang karena tidak punya sayap tapi BISA terbang tinggi dengan naik pesawat.Setelah dijelaskan oleh si B, maka si A berterimakasih dan Si B membalas “sama2“

Kesimpulan:

Can we fly like them? (Asking Ability, menanyakan kemampuan manusia bisa terbang seperti burung)We can't fly like them [birds], because we don't have wings (Responding Disability, merespon ketidakmampuan manusia terbang seperti burung karena manusia tidak punya sayap)But we can fly very high by a plane. (Responding Ability, respon kemampuan yg diberikan bahwa manusia bisa terbang tinggi tapi dengan naik pesawat)

26. Buatlah dialog tentang expression ability and willingness


Ability adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kemampuan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah can, could (past) dan managed to. Berikut ini adalah formula untuk menyatakan ability dalam bentuk present tense:

1. Is/am/are able to + main verb

Thomas is able to play the piano.Katy is not able to play the piano.

2. Can + main verb

Katy can play the flute.Thomas can't play the flute.

Willingness adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kesediaan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah will, would (past). Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat untuk menyatakan willingness dalam bentuk present tense:

We’ll see you tomorrow.Perhaps dad will led me the car.

Contoh dialog:

Katy: Can you help me with my piano practice tomorrow?

Thomas: I'm afraid I can't make it for Sunday. What about next week on Saturday. I will be free and then I can help you whole day.

Katy: Sure, that will be great! Thanks Thomas.

Penjelasan:

Here are several ways of saying the ability and inability.

ability:

can (natural skill: she can sing)will be able to (future skill: she will be able to see you tomorrow)was able to (past ability with some effort: they were able to win despite his goal keeper was injured)

inability:

can't (natural inability)won't be able to (future inability)wasn't able to (past inability even with some efforts)

Here are several ways of saying the ability and inability.

ability:

can (natural skill: she can sing)

will be able to (future skill: she will be able to see you tomorrow)

was able to (past ability with some effort: they were able to win despite his goal keeper was injured)

inability:

can't (natural inability)

won't be able to (future inability)

wasn't able to (past inability even with some efforts)

40. Dialog pendek ability dan wilingnes


Ability ada suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kemampuan untuk melakukan sesuatu. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah can, could, able.

Untuk penjelasan mengenai Willingness bisa di cek pada tautan di bawah.

 

Expressing ability and inability:

can, can’t, could, couldn’t, able, not able

 

Present: can, able

Is/am/are able to + main verb. Examples:

Jerry is able to play the guitar.Kitty is not able to play the guitar.

Can + main verb. Examples:

Kitty can play the violin.Thomas can’t play the violin.

Past: could, was/were + able to

Was/ were able to + main verb. Examples:

Jerry was able to play the guitar when he was a boy.Kitty was not able to play piano when she was a girl.

Could + verb. Examples:

When Erlin was in Germany, she could speak German.When Erlin was very young, she couldn’t speak German.
