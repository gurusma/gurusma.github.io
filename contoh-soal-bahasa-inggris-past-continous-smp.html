2. bagaimana rumus bahasa inggris simple past continous


Rumus Past Continuous Tense

Untuk membentuk kalimatnya, kita harus menerapkan rumus bentuk lampau dari be + present participle (-ing) pada kata kerja utama.
BentukRumus Past Continuous TenseContoh KalimatPositif
(+)Subjek + be (was/were) + verb (-ing) + …She was waiting for you yesterday
They were discussing my birthday party
Negatif
(-)Subjek +be (was/were) + not + verb (-ing) + …She was not waiting for you yesterday
They were not discussing my birthday party
Interrogatif
(?)Be (was/were) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?Was she waiting for you yesterday?
Were they discussing my birthday party?

(+) subjek+be(was/were)+verb(-ing)+...
(-) subjek+be(was/were)+not+verb(-ing)+...

12. Udahan dari bahasa Inggris dari ayah sedang mmembaca koran sekarang.ke past continous tense


Jawaban:

Bahasa Inggris dari ayah sedang membaca koran sekarang adalah father is reading newspaper now.

Past continuous tense dari ayah sedang membaca koran adalah father was reading newspaper.

Penjelasan:

Father is reading newspaper now merupakan kalimat menggunakan present continuous tense. Rumus dari present continuous tense adalah

subject + am/is/are + verb -ing + object.

Keterangan waktu yang digunakan pada present continuous tense adalah now.

Father was reading newspaper merupakan kalimat menggunakan past continuous tense. Rumus dari past continuous tense adalah

subject + was/were + verb -ing + object.

Past continuous tense biasanya diikuti dengan kejadian lain di waktu lampau yang terjadi bersamaan. Contoh:

Father was reading newspaper when I came.

22. Tugas untuk bahasa inggris tolong buat 5 kalimat (+-?) Dari past continous tense​


Soal :

Make 5 sentence by using past continuous tense!

Jawaban:

(+) : i was sleeping when you phoned me.

(Saya sedang tidur ketika anda menelpon saya)

(+) : they were swimming wile i was studying.

(Mereka sedang berenang ketikan saya sedang belajar)

(-) : he wasn't studying while we were listening to the radio

(Dia tidak belajar ketika kita sedang mendengarkan radio)

(?) : were you playing the piano as her mother entered the room?

(Apakah anda sedang bermain piano ketika ibunya masuk?)

(-?) : weren't Nora and Arief crying when grandma sang a song?

(Bukankan Mora dan Arief sedang menangis ketika nenek menyanyikan sebuah lagu)

Penjelasan:

Past Continuous tens dipergunakan untuk menyatakan suatu perbuatan atau peristiwa yang sedang berlangsung pada waktu lampau ketika kejadian lain terjadi.

Rumus :

S + To be (was,were) + V I (ing) + ...

My Aunt's Office

My name is Alicia. I am spending my holiday in Jakarta. I stay with my aunt. Her name is Farah Adusi. She is 22 years old. She works as an administrative staff of a Production House in Jakarta. I visited her office last week. Her office is unique. I will tell you about it.

Aunt Farah shares an office space with one of her colleagues, Miss Destri. She also works as an administrative staff of the company. They are very close friend. They went to the same high school. My aunt's office is a room with size about 4 m long and 3 m wide, with light grey paint covers all the walls. When you enter the room, you will find a big window right across the room. In the morning, it is showered by the morning sun light because it faces the east direction. The room is bright so that the ceiling lights are not needed much during the day.

There is a long wooden desk in the middle of the room with four chairs well arranged surround it. My aunt bought it when she traveled to Jepara two years ago. Two personal computers are put on the table. There is no cubicle in the office. Aunt Farah and Miss Destri sit in rows of seats face north in front of their own personal computers. There are a printer and a cordless telephone in the free space between them. There is also a small metal tray on the table in which they put the stationery neatly. In one side of the wall, there is a huge whiteboards. It is full of notes of the deadlines about some projects which they are working on.

Miss Destri brought a flashy blue clock that is hanged on the wall next to the whiteboard. In the corner of the room there is a cabinet used to store many files. Right next to the cabinet, there is a small wooden table which is covered with white and blue stripes tablecloth. There is a beautiful flower vase with a huge red roses bouquet on it. Near the window, there is a pile of books and work portfolios which are kept neatly on the shelf.

My aunt's office is not too large but it is clean and well organized. The air is always fresh and cool because there is an air conditioner installed on the wall. I like how my aunt arrange her office. I will go there again tomorrow. Aunt Farah and Miss Destri will treat me a mini tour to the whole building and buy me a lunch in the office cafe. It will be so much fun.

40. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.
