6. discribing about wage rudolf supratman. tolong...


Wage Rudolf Soepratman is the author of the national anthem Indonesia Raya which has been confirmed as the national hero of Indonesia. WR Soepratman was one of the sons of a sergeant in Battalion VIII named Senen. WR Soepratman was born in Jatinegara, Jakarta on 9 March 1903. He graduated from elementary school in Jakarta. In 1914, WR Soepratman joined his elder sister Roekijem to Makassar.
 
There he was educated and financed by Roekijem's husband, Willem van Eldik. Soepratman then studied Dutch at night school for three years, then proceeded to Normaalschool in Makassar to complete. When he was 20, he became a teacher

Wage Rudolf Soepratman adalah pengarang lagu kebangsaan Indonesia Raya yang telah dikukuhkan sebagai pahlawan nasional Indonesia.
