1 ) do you think i apply for this job

Sebelum menyusun dan menterjemahkan kalimat di atas, mari kita pelajari mengenai SHOULD dan SHOULD HAVE.

SHOULD adalah salah satu jenis auxiliary verb, digunakan untuk memberikan saran, rekomendasi atau nasehat. dalam terjemahan bebas Indonesia artinya seharusnya atau sebaiknya. SHOULD digunakan dalam bentuk present tense dan future tense.

                                    Subject + Should +Verb 1

Contoh penggunaan should :

You should read the safety regulation before start the project.Should I tell her that I can't come tomorrow? Yes, you should.You should see a dentist to check you teeth.Anna should help her mom.

SHOULD HAVE digunakan untuk membicarakan kejadian di masa lampau dimana kejadian tersebut tidak terjadi.

                             Subject+ Should have+ Verb 3

Contoh penggunaan should have :

You should have worn a raincoat.Moira shouldn't have eaten so much spicy food.I should have set the alarm clock.Rita should have written a letter to her friend.

Susun kalimat dibawah ini menjadi kalimat yang benar dan terjemahkan

Do you think I should apply for this job? (Apakah menurutmu aku sebaiknya melamar pekerjaan ini ?)Yes, I think you should obey your parents.Those children shouldn't be playing online games at this hour. They should be at school. (Anak-anak itu seharusnya tidak main games online pada jam-jam ini. Mereka seharusnya berada di sekolah).I feel sick, I shouldn't have eaten so much spicy food at the party. (Saya sakit perut, saya seharusnya tidak makan yang pedas-pedas di pesta)What should have I done to win the match? (Apa yang seharusnya aku lakukan untuk memenangkan pertandingan?)

16. Rearrange the jumbled words below to form good sentences 1. Shouldnt you smoke in this room 2. He drink? Should much water 3. They take some medicines should 4. She should ? To the hospital go


Re-Arrange the jumbled words below to form good sentences!

Jawaban:

1.) You shouldn't smoke in this room.

2.) Should he drink much water? (Karna ada tanda tanya (?)) atau

He should drink much water (Kalau tidak ada tanda tanya)

3.) They should take some medicines.

4.) Should she go to the hospital? (Karna ada tanda tanya (?)) atau

She should go to the hospital (Kalau tidak ada tanda tanya.)

Penjelasan:

1.) Artinya, Kamu tidak boleh/harus merokok di ruangan ini. (You shouldn't smoke in this room.)

2.) Artinya, Haruskah dia minum banyak air? (Should he drink much water?)

Dia harus minum banyak air. (He should drink much water)

3.) Artinya, Mereka harus minum obat. (They should take some medicines.)

4.) Artinya, Haruskah dia pergi ke rumah sakit? (Should she go to the hospital?)

Dia harus pergi ke rumah sakit. (She should go to the hospital)
