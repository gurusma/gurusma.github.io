Irregular adalah kata kerja dalam Past tense (V2) dan Past Participle (V3)

Contoh Verb: Tangkap/menangkap

Catch V1 => Caught V2/V3

Contoh Past Tense:

(+) S + V2 + O

=> The cat caught the mouse yesterday

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

=> The cat did not catch the mouse yesterday

(?) did + S + v1 + O?

=> did the cat catch the mouse yesterday?

Catatan:

Pada kalimat Positive (+) menggunakan V2 “caught”

Sedangkan pada kalimat Negative (-) dan interrogative (?) menggunakan V1 “catch” karena sudah ada “did”

verb ing itu adalah digunakan untuk kata kerja yang sedang kamu lagi saat ini atau sampai ini
verb ed itu digunakan untuk kata kerja yang sudah terjadi kalau verb + ing digunakan untuk kejadian saat ini atau sekarang
sedangkan verb +ed untuk kejadian yang sudah berlangsung (lampau)

7. 1.buatlah 5 kalimat menggunakan regular verb dan irreguler verb semua kalimat menggunakan positif (+),negatif (-) dan tanya (?)​


Jawaban:

Irregular Verbs:

1. Go - Went

(+) She went to school yesterday.

(-) She did not go to school yesterday.

(?) Did she go to school yesterday?

2. Read - Read

(+) I read an English book a few minutes ago.

(-) I did not read an English book a few minutes ago.

(?) Did I read an English book a few minutes ago?

3. Drink - Drank

(+) She drank milk last night.

(-) She did not drink milk last night.

(?) Did she drink milk last night?

Regular Verbs:

4. (+) A Beautiful girl smiled at me two minutes ago.

(-) A Beautiful girl did not smile at me two minutes ago.

(?) Did a Beautiful girl smile at me two minutes ago?

5. (+) He listened to music yesterday.

(-) He did not listen to music yesterday.

(?) Did he listen to music yesterday?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Past Tense dengan menggunakan kata kerja beraturan (Regular Verbs) dan kata kerja yang tidak beraturan (Irregular Verbs) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?
