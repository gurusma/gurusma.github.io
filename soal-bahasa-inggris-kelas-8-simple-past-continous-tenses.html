10. tentukan tenses : 1. simple present tense 2. present continous tense 3. simple past tense 4. simple future tense


Jawaban:

1. Simple present tense:

- The study results offer the first proof of Euphorbia hirta L. (he action of offering proof is happening in the present)

2. Present continuous tense:

- The formulation features the ideal physical and stability characteristics of mouthwash.

(the formulation is currently possessing the characteristics)

3. Simple past tense:

- Clinical use of the formulated mouthwash must be explored in future research.

(the exploration of clinical use happened in the past)

4. Simple future tense:

- Clinical use of the formulated mouthwash must be explored in future research.

(the action of exploring clinical use will happen in the future)

3. Simple Past Tense

You’re right, simple past tense ini adalah kebalikan dari simple present tense. Dalam hal ini, simple past tense digunakan untuk menceritakan suatu kejadian, kebiasaan, menyatakan fakta, atau situasi yang terjadi di masa lampau. Tense ini menggunakan kata kerja bentuk kedua atau lampau. Kata keterangan waktu yang biasa digunakan dalam tense ini adalah yesterday, two days ago, dan masih banyak lagi.

1. Simple Present Tense

Simple present tense adalah bentuk tense yang paling umum digunakan dalam kehidupan sehari-hari. Biasanya, simple present tense digunakan untuk mengungkapkan fakta, menceritakan suatu kejadian, atau kebiasaan yang terjadi di masa sekarang.

2. Present Continuous Tense

Berbeda dengan simple present tense, present continuous tense digunakan untuk menceritakan kejadian yang sedang berlangsung waktu kamu berbicara. Selain itu, tense yang satu ini juga bisa digunakan untuk menceritakan rencana di masa depan. Untuk tenses ini, terdapat perubahan bentuk verb dengan penambahan –ing pada bagian akhir. Salah satu kata keterangan waktu yang banyak digunakan dalam tense ini adalah now.

Namun, selain itu, tense yang satu ini juga bisa digunakan untuk menceritakan keputusan di masa depan yang spontan atau mengekspresikan prediksi yang berhubungan dengan waktu yang akan datang

13. pengertian present perfect continous tenses dalam bahasa inggris


The present perfect continuous tense (also known as the present perfect progressivet ense) shows that something started in the past and is continuing at the present time.

The present perfect continuous is formed using the construction:
has/have been + verb base+ -ing.

example: I have been singing for 5 hours.

22. tolong buatkan cerita bahasa inggris yang mengandung simple future tense,simple past future tense,dan present continous tense


Explanation:

- Simple future tenseThis tense is used to tell about an action that will be happened in the future.The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example, I will go to Bali next month.

- Simple past future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which would be happened in the past. This tense is mostly used to show wish or hope.
The structure: S + would + V1 + O
For example, I wish he would forgive me.

- Present continuous tenseThis tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + V-ing + O
For example, She is reading a book in the library.

25. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.

32. jelaskan past continous past perfect tenses future tenses dan past future tenses


past continuous : menyatakn suatu perbuatan yg SEDANG berlangsung pd waktu lampau ketika perstiwa lain juga terjadi
rumusnya : S + was/were + Verb-ing
e.g : i was reading a book when mom watched tv last night (aku sedang membaca buku ketika ibu nonton tv)

past perfect : menyatakn suatu perbuatan yg SUDAH SELESAI dilakukan pd waktu lampau
rumusnya : S + had + been + ...  /  S + had + Verb 3
e.g : i had been at school when you went to kupang(aku sudah berada di sekolah ketika kau pergi ke kupang)

future tense : menyatakn suatu perbuatan yg AKAN DILAKUKAN pd waktu yg akan datang
rumusnya : S + shall/will + verb 1  / S + shall/will + be + ...
e.g : i will go to surabaya tomorrow (aku akan pergi ke surabaya besok)

past future : menyatakn suatu perbuatan yg AKAN DATANG DILAKUKAN pd waktu lampau
rumusnya : S + should/would + be +.... / S + should/would + Verb 1
e.g : you would give us book the week before (kau akan memberi kami buku minggu sebelumnya)

Belajar Bahasa Inggris Dari Lirik Lagu

Kalimat yang akan kita bahas dalam lagu di atas adalah :

I should've seen before.

Kalimat di atas digunakan untuk mengungkapkan penyesalan di waktu lampau. Agar lebih jelas mari kita lihat fakta yang sebenarnya terjadi di waktu lampau.

Fakta :

I didn't see.

Penyesalannya :

I should've seen before.

Mari kita bandingkan lagi contoh yang lain!

Fakta :

I loved her too much.

Penyesalannya :

I shouldn't have loved her too much.

Dari segi arti, fakta selalu bertentangan dengan penyesalannya. Kalau faktanya dalam bentuk negatif, penyesalannya positif dan sebaliknya.

Dari segi pola kalimat, faktanya dalam bentuk Simple Past Tense, yaitu Subyek + Kata Kerja II atau Subyek + did + not + Kata Kerja I.

Sedangkan penyesalannya dalam bentuk pola Subyek + should (not) + have + Kata Kerja III .

Sekali lagi, jika kita melakukan sesuatu di masa lampau, lalu kita menyesalinya, gunakan pola Subyek + should + have + Kata Kerja III.

Sebagai penutup, mari kita lihat penyesalan seseorang telah menelantarkan orang tuanya ketika hidup dan faktanya di dalam kurung.

I should have seen my parents more often. ( I didn't see my parents often. )

I should have taken care of them more patiently. ( I didn't take care of them more patiently.)

I should have given them what they loved. ( I didn't give them what they loved .)

36. bagaimana rumus bahasa inggris simple past continous


Rumus Past Continuous Tense

Untuk membentuk kalimatnya, kita harus menerapkan rumus bentuk lampau dari be + present participle (-ing) pada kata kerja utama.
BentukRumus Past Continuous TenseContoh KalimatPositif
(+)Subjek + be (was/were) + verb (-ing) + …She was waiting for you yesterday
They were discussing my birthday party
Negatif
(-)Subjek +be (was/were) + not + verb (-ing) + …She was not waiting for you yesterday
They were not discussing my birthday party
Interrogatif
(?)Be (was/were) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?Was she waiting for you yesterday?
Were they discussing my birthday party?

(+) subjek+be(was/were)+verb(-ing)+...
(-) subjek+be(was/were)+not+verb(-ing)+...

1. simple present : object + to be (am, is, are) + verb 3 + subject

2. present continous: object + to be (am, is, are) + being + verb 3 + subject

3. simple past : object + to be (was, were) + verb 3 + subject

4. past continous : object + to be (was, were) + being + verb 3 + subject

5. present perfect : object + to be (has,have) + verb 3 + subject

6. past perfect : object + to be (had) + been + verb 3 + subject

7. future : object + will be + verb 3 + subject

8. future continous : object + will be + verb 1 (+ ing) + subject

9. future perfect : object + will + to be (have) + verb 3 + subject
