10. ubahlah ke dalam simple past beserta artinya​


1. Bring = Brought (membawa)

2. Catch = caught (menangkap)

3. Drink = drank (minum)

4. Feel = felt (merasa)

5. Get = got (mendapat)

6. Hear = heard (mendengar)

7. Know = knew (mengetahui)

8. Leave = left (meninggalkan)

9. Come = came (datang)

10. Buy = bought (membeli)

Penjelasan

Kata kerja di atas termasuk irregular verb, kata kerja tidak beraturan.

1. Bring:
Simple past: Brought.
Meaning: Come to a place with (someone or something).
2. Catch:
Simple past: Caught.
Meaning: Capture (a person or animal that tries or would try to escape).
3. Drink:
Simple past: Drank.
Meaning: Take (a liquid) into the mouth and swallow.
4. Feel:
Simple past: Felt.
Meaning: Be aware of (a person or object) through touching or being touched.
5. Get:
Simple past: Got.
Meaning: Come to have or hold (something); receive.
6. Hear:
Simple past: Heard.
Meaning: Perceive with the ear the sound made by (someone or something).
7. Know:
Simple past: Knew.
Meaning: Be absolutely certain or sure about something.
8. Leave:
Simple past: Left.
Meaning: Go away from.
9. Come:
Simple past: Came.
Meaning: Arrive at a specified place.
10. Buy:
Simple past: Bought.
Meaning: Obtain in exchange for payment.

11. Tulis rumus simple past tense dan simple present tense Beserta Contohnya


Simple past tense:

subject + V2 + O

ex:
(+) I went to primary school in England.
(-) My grandmother didn't like cats.
(?) Did you get my letter?


Simple present tense:

Subject + V(use s at the back if the subject are he, she, it) + O

ex:
(+) She wears jeans all the time.
(-) He doesn't enjoy it.
(?) Does Jack smoke?@Answer:

1) Simple Present
(+) S + V1(s/es) + O/C => She reads a book.
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C => She doesn't read a book.
(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O/C? => Does she read a book?

2) Simple Past
(+) S + V2 + O/C => We cook a cake.
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O/C => We didn't cook a cake.
(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C? => Did we cook a cake?

13. pengertian simple past tense beserta contoh


Simple Past adalah Tenses yg menunjukkan kejadian/peristiwa di waktu/masa lampau dan selesai di masa/waktu lampau juga.

Simple Past yg berbentuk Verbal (yg ada kata kerja/verb)

(+) Subject + V2 + Object

= I play a ball yesterday

(Saya bermain sebuah bola kemarin)

(-) S + did not + V1 + Object

= I did not play a ball yesterday

(Saya tidak bermain bola kemarin)

(?) did I play a ball yesterday

(Apa saya bermain sebuah bola kemarin?)

Simple Past bentuk Nominal (yg tak ada kerja/ tanpa verb)

(+) Subject + was/were + adjective/adverb/noun

= I was so happy yesterday  

(saya sangat bahagia kemarin)

(-) S + was/were + not + object (adj/adv/noun)

= I was not happy yesterday

(Saya tak bahagia kemarin)

(?) was/were + S + O (adj/adv/noun)

= was I happy yesterday?

Time signal (waktu yg menunjukkan Past Tense)

= yesterday, last, ago dll

18. bagaimana rumus simple past tance beserta contohnya


Simple Past Tense adalah suatu "Tense" atau kalimat yang menjelaskan sesuatu yang telah terjadi di masa lampau, dan sudah berakhir pada masa kini

Rumus : Subject + Verb2 + O

Contoh : I opened the door (Saya telah membuka pintu)
possitive (+) , negative (-) , interrogative (?)

contohnya =
(+) = I want to jogjakarta with my friends last week
(-)= I don't wanna go to jogjakarta with my friends last week
(?)=whether me and my friends will go to jogjakarta last week ?

Simple Past Tense

Apa itu Simple Past Tense?

Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat sederhana yang menunjukkan kegiatan/tindakan yang terjadi di masa lalu.

Rumus Simple Past Tense :

Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Subject + Did/Was/Were + Not + Verb 1 + Object

Did + Subject + Infitinitive Verb + Object + ?

Was/Were + Subject + Adjective/Adverb + ?

Contoh :

- Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Example : I met my cousin last week.

She went to the hospital two days ago.

- Subject + Did/Was/Were + Not + Verb 1 + Object

Example : I didn't go to school three days ago.

She wasn't play with her friends last night.

- Did + Subject + Infinitive Verb + Object

Example : Did you go to the market alone?

Did she find something to eat?

- Was/Were + Subject + Adjective + Adverb

Example : Was Alya sad because of him?

Were they bored at home?
