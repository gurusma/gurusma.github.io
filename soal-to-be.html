36. Scepatny tlong jwb soal ini.Petunjuk: Write your sentences here!Use be verb+ing and be going to ​


1. Mr. Zein is having lunch with a client now.

Mr. Zein is going to have lunch with a client Next Wednesday.

2. Mr. Zein is presenting Company Product to MNC now.

Mr. Zein is going to present Company Product to MNC managers at 9 tonight.

3. Mr. Zein is seeing a dentist now.

Mr. Zein is going to see a dentist Next Week.

4. Mr. Zein is having a conference call with foreign client now.

Mr. Zein is going to have a conference call with foreign client Next Thursday morning.

5. Mr. Zein is having a meeting with staff now.

Mr. Zein is going to have a meeting with staff Next two week.

Penjelasan:

Present Continuous Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk kejadian yang sedang terjadi.

(+) S + be + verb 1 + -ing

(-) S + be + not + verb 1 + -ing

(?) be + S + verb 1 + -ing

Future "be going to" adalah tense yang digunakan untuk kejadian yang akan terjadi di masa depan.

(+) S + be going to + verb 1

(-) S + be + not + going to + verb 1

(?) be + S + going to + verb 1
