6. mention 2 expression of compliments and 2 respond of compliments​


║COMPLIMENT EXPRESSION║

[tex]Answer[/tex]

First Compliment Expression & responding

x = I love the way you presented your thesis this afternoon. You are such a brilliant girl.

y = Thanks. But it's nothing really great. I just wanted to show my argument.

Second Compliment Expression & responding

x = It was unbelievable ! How did you do that?! You are very talented.

y = Thank you for saying so. It's just my hobby.

[tex]Explanation[/tex]

Compliment is used to reveal praise/admiration on people's general appearance, new look, when we meet him/her for the first time, something people did, and something people have in order to evoke someone' spirit to be better in the future.

27. Dialog bahasa inggris dengan kata certainty uncertainty,expressing admiration or compliments,asking for repetition? Dengan artinya+judul nya,terima kasih sebelum nya


*Certainty uncertainty
A: Do you know the answer of number 2?
B: Yes, it's 'A'
A: Are you sure?
B: Of course!
A: Is the answer of number 5, 'B'?
B: I'm not too sure about that.

A: Apakah kamu tahu jawaban no.2?
B: Ya, itu adalah A
A: Apakah kamu yakin?
B: Tentu saja!
A: Apakah jawaban no. 5 adalah B?
B: Aku tidak terlalu yakin tentang itu.

*Expressing compliments
A: Whose bag is this?
B: It's mine.
A: Oh, really?? That's really beautiful! By the way, Where did you get it?
B: Thank you! My mom bought it for me yesterday.

A: Punya siapa tas ini?
B: Ini milikku.
A: Oh, benarkah?? Itu sangat cantik! Ngomong-ngomong, di mana kamu mendapatkannya?
B: Terima kasih! Ibuku membelikannya untukku kemarin.

*Asking for Repetition
A: Mr. James, could you please repeat your explanation? I don't really understand it.
B: Of course!

A: Pak James, bisakah Anda mengulangi penjelasannya? Saya tidak terlalu mengerti.
B: Tentu saja!
