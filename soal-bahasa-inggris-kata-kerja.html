3. BAgi mana menjawab soal bahasa inggris ini mencari kata kerja jawabannya apa


Dengan mencari kata kerja ke-2

biasanya ada di pertengahan buku kamus bahasa inggris jika tdk ada kata kata yg ingin dicari tdk ada di pertengahan kamus maka anda bisa menambahakan 'ed'

Contoh:

1. Turn menjadi turned

2. Stay menjadi stayed

3. Ask menjadi asked

4. Mix menjadi mixed

5. Mop menjadi moped

Tdk semua kata ada di pertengahan kamus

Rumus kalimat ber kata kerja adalah

(+) Subjek + kata kerja kedua + objek

(-) subjek + did not + kata kerja pertama + objek

(?) Did + subjek + kata kerja pertama + objek?

Contoh:

(+) i went to Banjarmasin yesterday

(-) Last sunday we played football

(?) Did i go to beach yesterday

During Ramadan, Muslims worldwide observe a month-long period of fasting, refraining from food and drink from dawn until sunset. (observe, fast, refrain)

They dedicate their time to acts of worship, including reciting the Quran, performing additional prayers, and seeking spiritual enlightenment. (dedicate, recite, perform, seek)

Charity is an integral part of Ramadan, with Muslims actively engaging in acts of giving, such as donating food, clothing, and money to the less fortunate. (engage, donate)

Mosques become bustling centers of activity during Ramadan, as Muslims gather for congregational prayers, participate in religious lectures, and strengthen their bond with the community. (gather, participate, strengthen)

At the end of Ramadan, Muslims celebrate the joyous occasion of Eid al-Fitr, marked by communal prayers, feasting, exchanging gifts, and expressing gratitude for the blessings received. (celebrate, mark, feast, exchange, express)

Aktivitas kegiatan terjadi di masa lampau sehingga kita menggunakan bentuk kalimat Active & Passive Voice dari Simple Past Tense.

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat.

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat.

Simple Past Tense:

Active Voice: (+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

Passive Voice: (+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-3 + Object

Modal Verbs yang digunakan juga dalam bentuk lampau:

(+) Subject + Could + Verb-1 + Object

(-) Fikry is not cooking fried rice.

(?) Is Fikry cooking fried rice?

(-) 9D students are not studying English right now.

(?) Are 9D students studying English right now?

3. (+) The girls are watching music concert.

(-) The girls are not watching music concert.

(?) Are The girls watching music concert?

4. (+) Rabbit is eating carrot at the moment.

(-) Rabbit is not eating carrot at the moment.

(?) Is Rabbit eating carrot at the moment?

Penjelasan:

(+) Subject + to be (am, is, are) + verb-ing + object.

(-) Subject + to be (am, is, are) + not + verb-ing + object.

(?) To be (am, is, are) + subject + verb-ing + object.
