14. contoh kalimat simple past,past continuous,past perfect,past p.continus,future, future continuous, future perfect,f.p cont,past future,p.f.continouse,past perfect continuous,​


Here are examples of each tense:

Simple Past:

I watched a movie last night.She cooked dinner for us yesterday.They visited their grandparents on the weekend.

Past Continuous:

I was studying when the phone rang.He was playing football while it was raining.We were talking about our plans for the weekend.

Past Perfect:

She had already finished her homework before dinner.They had visited the museum before it closed.He had gone to the store before it started raining.

Past Perfect Continuous:

She had been studying for three hours before taking a break.They had been waiting for the bus for half an hour before it arrived.He had been working on the project all day before submitting it.

Future:

I will go to the beach next weekend.She will visit her family during the holidays.They will attend the concert next month.

Future Continuous:

I will be studying for my exams tomorrow.He will be working on his presentation all morning.They will be traveling to Europe next year.

Future Perfect:

She will have finished her project by the end of the week.They will have graduated from college in a few months.He will have completed the course before starting the new job.

Future Perfect Continuous:

She will have been studying English for two years by next summer.They will have been living in the city for five years by next month.He will have been working at the company for ten years by his retirement.

Past Future:

She said she would help me with my project tomorrow.They promised they would call me later in the evening.He told me he would come to the party next week.

Past Future Continuous:

She said she would be studying all night for the exam.They promised they would be working on the project until it's finished.He told me he would be traveling for the next two weeks.

I hope these examples help clarify each tense for you!


15. past continuous tense (+),(-),(?)preset continuous tense (+),(-),(?)future continuous tense (+),(-),(?)contoh:​


Jawaban:

Past continuous tense

(+) I was watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(-) I was not watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(?) Was I watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home  last night?

Preset continuous tense

(+) Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment.

(-) Our school principal is not giving his speech at the moment.

(?) Is our school principal giving his speech at the moment?

Future continuous tense

(+) I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(-) I will not be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(?) Will I be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning?

Penjelasan:

Ketiga tenses ini mengandung kata "continuous" (berkelanjutan/terus-menerus) yang mengandung makna bahwa yang dikerjakan itu masih berlangsung.

Dalam Past continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik di masa lampau. (Dalam contoh si atas adalah "when my mother came home last night" (ketika ibuku pulang tadi malam). Jadi kalimat ini bermakna bahwa ketika ibu pulang tadi malam, saya sedang menonton film . Jadi kegiatan menonton itu masih berlangsung ketika ibu pulang, tadi malam (masa lampau)

Dalam Present continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik masa kini atau sekarang (yaitu pada titik waktu ketika sipenutur mengucapkan kata ini).

Contoh kalimat di atas: Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment. (kepala sekolah saat ini sedang berpidato).

Skenarionya misalnya kamu sedang ada di sekolah mendengarkan pidato kepala sekolah, lalu kamu ditelpon mama, lalu kamu bilang "Mom, our school principal is giving his speech at the moment"

Dalam Future continuous tense, kita membayangkan/memperkirakan bahwa di satu  titik waktu di masa depan sesuatu sedang terjadi/dikerjakan.

Dalam contoh di atas: I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow moring.

Artinya besok pagi pas waktu kamu tiba, saya akan sedang melakukan sesuatu yaitu menyiapkan laporan.

17. Berikan Contoh paragraf Future tense :- Mengandung Simple future tense, future continuous, Future perfect, future perfect continuous- terdiri dari 3 paragraf- 1 paragraf (4-5 baris atau lebih)Tolong bantu jawab ya...Boleh tentang rencana liburan/cita- cita setelah lulus/yang lainnya..


Answer: 

Future tense is a tense which is used to indicate an action which will happen in the future. Future tense is divided into 4, they are:

- Simple future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which will happen at a specific time in the future.
The structure: S + will + V1 + O

- Future continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which will be in progress at a specific time in the future.
The structure: S + will + be + V-ing + O

- Future perfect tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which will be completely done before another time in the future.
The structure: S + will + have + V3 + O

- Future perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to emphasize the duration of an action that will be in progress before another event in the future.
The structure: S + will + have + been + V-ing + O


Here's the example of their usage:
Next weekend my cousin, Tasya, will visit Surabaya for 3 days. She will arrive at 11 pm on Friday. She will stay at my home. But I will not be able to pick her up because I will sleep at 10 pm. I will be sleeping when she arrives here. But, it's not a big deal because we can spend our weekend together. She will have visited many places in Surabaya by the time she goes home.

On the first day, we will go to Surabaya Zoo at 10 o'clock in the morning. We will have eaten breakfast by the time we arrive at the zoo. We will walk around and take many pictures. My sister will join us too. She will meet at 12 pm because she has another business on that day. My cousin and I will have been walking around the zoo for 2 hours when my sister arrives. After that, we will go to Galaxy mall. We will be there until dinner time and will go home at 8 pm.

On the second day, we will visit Tugu Pahlawan. After that, we will go to Tunjungan Plaza mall to have lunch. My mother will catch us up after lunchtime. We will have had lunch by the time my mother arrives. She will take us to Zangrandi Ice Cream. In the evening, we will go to Surabaya Night Carnival at 6. We will play many rides there. My sister will catch us up at 7. So, we will have been playing for 1 hour when my sister arrives. On the last day, we will not go anywhere because my cousin has to go to the airport in the morning.

34. future continuous tense itu contohnya gimna?​


Future Continuous Tense

Penjelasan

Future Continuous Tense atau Future Progressive Tense merupakan bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang akan sedang berlangsung pada suatu waktu di masa yang akan datang dengan keterangan waktu yang spesifik.

Contoh

Positif Tense

Mikaela will be running in a marathon race this Sunday morning.I will be staying with my sister for a week.I will be eating at 7 o’clock tonight.I shall returning the book to the library this evening.He will be coming to my birthday party tomorrow.

Negative Tense

I will not be staying with my sister for a week.Erika will not be competing against Mikaela in the race when the race starts.Due to the new regulations of our office, we shall not be eating in our room starts from tomorrow morning.She will not be coming to your farewell party next week.He promise will be not playing games for 1 month.

Interogative Tense

Will Sunny and Rain be watching Mikaela race this Sunday evening?Will I be sleeping in this room?Will you be coming to Susi’s farewell party?Shall you be returning this tray to that counter, please?Will James be exercising at the gym?

(+) S + will + V1 + O/C

Future Continous

Future Perfect Continous
