10. Menyusun kata bahasa inggris : 1. Passed/ paul/ easly/ the exam 2. Every day/ do/the same thing/we 3. Did you buy/ in england/ that jacket?


1. Passed/ paul/ easily/ the exam
= Paul passed the exam easily
(paul melewati ujiannya dengan mudah)

2. Every day/ do/the same thing/we
= we do the same thing every day
(kami melakukan hal yg sama setiap hari)

3. Did you buy/ in england/ that jacket?
= Did you buy that jacket in England?
(apa kau beli jaket itu di inggris?)

13. soal menjodohkan bahasa inggris tentang recount text about sport events


Last year, my school and some schools collaborated with health department to held sports events. It is held close to the Guesan ulun museum.

when the day come i can see crowds with many students from other schools from afar, so to not get apart we had to group together.

My group and I looked around
for a bit and then parted in half, I grouped together with my friends to the registration booth, to join the game. I choose too join the rope skipping game and hula hoop game because the other games is full of participants so that’s the only option left.

After we finished the registration we group back with our whole group in the hall. we get education about the importance of exercise regularly, and
And what kind of food is good for your body.

After we finished we went to the game places, some people in the group just watch from afar and some of them go inside the arena to participate in the games,and I’m included.
When it’s my turn to play the hula hoop I lost within 3 seconds. It’s a bit embarrassing but i already knew it will eventually happen, so I wear a mask.but it turned out different with the rope skipping game. I win!
The reason why i join 2 games is simple by joining a game you already gained point but if you win a game you get more points! And you can exchange it for tote bags,keychain,and plushy! I exchanged my point for a keychain with a mini plushies on it it’s so cute.

after a day full of activity i return home by bus.
it was great, I have seen many students from different schools, learning something new and winning a game in the same time, it might be a bit embarrassing for the hula hoop game but it will always be a precious memory that I’ll always cherish.

Maaf kalau ada yg salah

Jawaban:

MY FAVORITE ROOM

My favorite room in the whole world is my own bedroom in my parents’ house.

It has everything that I want in this life: a comfortable bed that faces a wide window on the east side of the room. So, when I wake up in the morning and swipe the window curtain, the light of sunrise enters the room little by little, creates a beautiful silhouette and warms the whole room. On the right corner of room near the window, there’s a study desk that was once my father’s. Next to that desk, there’s a white wardrobe where I put all of my clothes.

In front of the wardrobe, at the other side of the room, there’s a yellow bookshelf where I put half of my book collection. I choose the collor yellow so it matches the collor of my bedroom wall. Next to the bookshelf, I have a dressing table. And next to that dressing table is where I put my guitar. It’s a fifteen-year-old guitar but it still sounds great and I love it so much.

36. the thing in the living room​


Jawaban:
1. framed photos of family members
2. table lamp
3. television/TV
4. cupboard/bookcase
5. telephone
6. sofa/couch
7. rug
8. coffee table/table
9. bag/workbag
10. curtains

Penjelasan dan terjemahan:
1. framed photos of family members adalah foto berbingkai anggota keluarga
2. table lamp adalah lampu meja
3. Television/TV(singkatannya) adalah televisi
4. cupboard adalah lemari, sedangkan bookcase adalah lemari buku (saya sarankan anda menggunakan cupboard, tetapi pilih pilihan anda)
5. telephone adalah telepon
6. sofa adalah istilah yang lebih santai untuk kata “sofa”, couch adalah istilah yang lebih formal untuk kata “sofa”
7. rug adalah carpet kecil atau tikar lantai
8. coffee table adalah sebuah meja kecil yang biasanya diletakan di ruang tamu atau ruang keluarga, dan fungsinya sebagai tempat meletakan minuman atau makanan kecil pada saat bercengkerama dengan keluarga maupun tamu sambil minum kopi atau teh, sedangkan table adalah meja.
9. bag adalah tas, workbag adalah tas kerja
10. curtains adalah tirai
