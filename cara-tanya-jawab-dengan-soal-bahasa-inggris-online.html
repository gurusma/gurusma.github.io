25. tolong dijawab pertanyaan soal bahasa inggrisnya


Jawab:
16. wakes up
17. prays
18. does not forget
19. watches

atau:
Everyday Daniel wakes up in the morning at 05.00.
= verb+s (wake+s) karena subject tunggal (Daniel)

after that, he prays subu.
= verb+s (pray+s) karena subject tunggal (he)

then he does not forget to have breakfast.
= menggunakan does karena subject tunggal (he)

he watches TV in his room.
= verb+es (watch+es) karena subject tunggal (he)

Catatan:
ada kata Everyday maka tenses yg digunakan adalah Present Tense,
(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O
(-) S + do/does + not V1 + O
(?) do/does + S + V1 + O?

===========================
Mapel: Bahasa Inggris
Materi: Present Tense

1. wakes up

2. prays Subu

18. don't forget

19. watches

27. Cara menjawab pertanyaan tentang umur dalam bahasa inggris


Cara menjawab pertanyaan tentang umur dalam bahasa Inggris adalah dengan memberikan jawaban berupa: “I am (your age) years old.”


Pembahasan

Seringkali ketika kita bertemu dengan seseorang, pertanyaan “How old are you” atau berapakah usiamu muncul dalam percakapan.


Sebagai contoh:

A: Hey, how old are you?

B: I’m 15 years old. How about you?

A: Me too!

Pelajaran: English
Kategori: questions

2. How many visitors in average are there in Taj Mahal recently? There are about 7-8 million people per year.
3. When did the construction of Taj Mahal finish? It finished in 1653.
4. What calligraphic writings were written on the actual tomb? The Ninety Nine Names of God in Moslem calligraphic writings were written there.
