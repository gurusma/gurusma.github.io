1. penggunaan modals could dalam bahasa inggris


Terdapat beberapa penggunaan modal verb ‘can’ dan ‘could’, diantaranya yaitu untuk menunjukan possibility (kemungkinan), request (permintaan), offering (penawaran), ability (kemampuan), dan lain sebagainya.

5. Buatlah masing masing 1 kalimat menggunakan Modal Auxiliary “Can, Could, May,and Might” dalam bahasa Indonesia dan di artikan kedalam bahasa Inggris ​


1. Can
You can buy anything with your money but you can’t buy love
(Kamu dapat membeli apapun dengan uangmu tapi kamu tidak dapat membeli cinta)

2. Could
You could run faster than me two years ago
(Kamu dapat berlari lebih cepat dari saya dua tahun lalu)

3. May
He may work out and eat healthy food everday
(Dia mungkim berlatih dan memakan makanan sehat setiap hari)

4. Might
If I have cleaned the room, might I play with my friend?
(Jika aku sudah membersihkan ruangan, bolehkan saya bermain dengan teman saya?)

Maaf kalau salah, semoga membantu:)

6. Apa perbedaan penggunaan kata could dan can dalam bahasa inggris?


Penjelasan:

could dan can artinya sama, yaitu dapat/bisa. penggunaan could utk past tense (Masa lampau). sedangkan can utk present /future tense.

7. tolong buatin soal pilihan ganda tentang meminta dan menawarkan barang dan jasa( could..?/ can i..) plis tolong bahasa inggris


Satu soal masing-masing? Boleh.

1. Meminta

A: "................... bring that book here, please?"
B: "Sure."
A: "Thank you!"

What is the best answer to fill the blank space above?

a. Could you
b. Been have you
c. Had me
d. You could
e. You would

Jawabannya: A

2. Menawarkan barang/jasa.

A: "Excuse me, ma'am. I am ........ offer you our stuff. It's incredibly useful, you will love it!"
B: "Let me see."
A: "There! A unique metal and sharp knife which you can also use as spoon as well!"
B: "Wow! It's magnificent."

What is the best answer to fill the blank space above?

a. come here
b. stay here
c. about to
d. get to
e. have to

Jawabannya: C

9. percakapan modals menggunakan bahasa inggris can/could/would/may/should


Can

A : Hello, B. How are you?
B : Hello, A. I'm fine, thanks
A : B, can you help me open the door, please?
B : Sure

Could

A : Hey, B. Could you teach me math?
B : Of course, A

May

A : Hey, B. May I sit next to you?
B : Yeah, of course

Should

A : Hmm... Should I do my homework at living room or at my room?
B : I think you should do it at your own room, A

35. sebutkan perbedaan penggunaan modal "can", "could", "be able" menggunakan bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

Be able to is possible in all tenses - but "can" is possible only in the present and "could" is possible only in the past for ability. ... So we use be able to when we want to use other tenses or the infinitive.
