A small boy was lying unconscious on the road, and the car that had hit him had already run away. Umar was confused and frightened. “is the boy dead?” Umar asked himself. However, the boy was still breathing. Umar could see that the boy had bruises all over his face and arms. Umar did not know what to do. Few cars passed along this road at this time of the day. The nearest house which he could go to for help was his own, but it would take ten minutes to walk there. Umar finally decided to stay with the boy until somebody came by. Time passed very slowly – so it seemed to Umar. The boy was still unconscious, and Umar prayed for the boy to regain consciousness. Umar sprang to his feet at the sound of an approaching car. He waved frantically at the driver to stop. Umar asked the driver,”You will help this boy, won,t you, Sir?” The driver was kind. He carried the injuried boy to the car. Umar went with them to the hospital. Later, the driver took Umar to the police station to report the accident. Umar had a lot to tell to his friends that day at school. All his friends though Umar was a very brave boy.”it was an unforgettable incident, wasn,t it?” They asked him. “Yes, it was, “Umar replied.”it was a terrifying experience, thought!”

31. bahasa inggris kelas 7 hal 129 k13mohon dijawab...trima kasih:)


3. Question: Lisa, how about your mother? What does she do?

   Answer: She is a housewife. She takes a good care of us and our house.

4. Question: What does your mother do, Udin?

   Answer: She's a surgeon. She performs operations on her patients.

5. Question: How about you, Edo? What do you do?

   Answer: 'm a student.

Pembahasan

Dialog diatas membahas mengenai pekerjaan dan profesi (Jobs and Professions). Beberapa pekerjaan yang disebutkan diatas adalah:

FarmerTeacherHousewifeSurgeonStudent

Beberapa Jobs and Profession lainnya dalam bahasa Inggris dan artinya:

Scientist = IlmuwanAccountant = akuntanPilot = pilotFisherman = nelayanDriver = supirResearcher = penelitiTechnician = teknisiLecturer = dosenLawyer = pengacaraShopkeeper = penjaga tokoTranslator = penterjemahBuilder = tukang bangunanPlumber = tukang pipaDancer = penariSinger = penyanyi

Untuk menanyakan mengenai profesi, phrase yang sering digunakan adalah:

What do you do?

What do you do for living?

What is your job?

What is your profession?

What does your father?

Bentuk-bentuk pertanyaan diatas menggunakan simple present tense, yaitu tense yang digunakan untuk kegiatan yang biasa dilakukan atau yang dilakukan secara reguler atau berulang kali.

37. buku bahasa inggris kelas 7 K13 halaman 109


Buku Bahasa Inggris kelas VII halaman 109 adalah menceritakan tentang kualitas benda-benda dan ruangan yang kamu miliki di sekolah. Pada perintah soal, kamu diminta memilih 10 objek (benda dan ruangan) untuk didiskusikan dan kemudian menceritakan kualitasnya.

Pembahasan

This is our school.

1. It is large, clean and green. There are  many shady places. (for example)

2. Our class have [number] classes. All of them are very large.

3. This is our class. It is very spacious. The facilities are complete.

4. There are two blackboards [or whiteboards] in front of our class. They are new and clean.

5. There are [number] tables in our class. They are old and messy. There are a lot of writings on it.

6. There are [number] chairs in our class. They are small but comfortable.

7. Next to my class, they are the restrooms. They are always clean and smell good in the morning. But, in the afternoon, they are sometimes dirty and make us dizzy with the smell.

8. There is a schoolyard in front of our class. It is very large and tidy. We can play football and basketball at there.

9. There is a canteen in our school. It is small but clean and neat.

10. There is a parking lot near the canteen. It is long and neat. We can put our bicycles at there.

11. There is a teacher's office in front of the parking lot. It is large but messy. The teachers' tables are big, so the pathway is very narrow. Our teachers put the books on their tables, so it doesn't look neat and uncomfortable.
