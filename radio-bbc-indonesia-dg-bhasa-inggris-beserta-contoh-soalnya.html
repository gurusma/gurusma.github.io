Grill chicken is very delicious. But if the skin is burned,the taste of the chicken will be bitter. And people usually marinate them with some sauce and spices to make the grilled chicken be more aromatic. Then, after the grilled chicken is finished, people usually dip them in some sweet soy sauce or some traditional indonesian chilli.

Ayam panggang sangatlah enak. Akan tetapi, jika kulit ayam tersebut gosong, rasanya akan menjadi pahit. 27. Contoh cerita liburan ke singapur bhasa inggris dan bahasa indonesia


Holiday in Singapore
During school holidays last time, my family and I went traveling to Singapore, I arrived in Singapore in the evening. We stayed in a hotel
The next day, we went to Universal studios in Sentosa Island. Just behind my hotel there are shops selling bus and entrance tickets to Universal Studios. My family and I had breakfast first before going there.
After breakfast, we came out and saw a bus heading to Universal studios had come. We then ran hastily to the ticket agent. In a hurried situation I said “I want to buy tickets”, because the seller speaks very fast, I’m difficult to understand what it means. She asked “Do you want to buy a bus ticket and entrance as well?” I replied, “Yes, I want to buy tickets for 3 adults and 1 child.” Then she told the price. We were shocked because the tickets were so expensive
We had fun playing at Universal Studios. We didn’t aware that it was afternoon. I’m very disappointed because we did not have enough time to play all the rides.
We had to go out and went back to hotel. I will never forget that holiday experience.

37. cara menggunakan radio dalam bahasa inggris beserta arti bahasa indonesia


1. First, plug the radio cable into the socket.
2. Then, turn on the radio by turning the knob.
3. After that, pull out the antenna.
4. Next, turn the channel knob to search for radio channels.
5. And then, adjust the antenna until the sound is clear.
6. Finally, adjust the volume to your liking.

Artinya :
1. Pertama, pasang kabel radio ke soket.
2. Kemudian, nyalakan radio dengan memutar kenop.
3. Setelah itu, cabut antena.
4. Selanjutnya, putar tombol saluran untuk mencari saluran radio.
5. Kemudian, atur antena sampai suara menjadi jelas.
6. Akhirnya, atur volume sesuai dengan keinginan anda.
