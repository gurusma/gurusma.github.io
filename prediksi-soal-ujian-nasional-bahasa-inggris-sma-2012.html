25. Hasil dari ∫ 3x√(3x^2 + 1) dx adalah … (Ujian Nasional Matematika Tahun 2012) **Super Intensif UN (Ujian Nasional) SMA/MA IPA , Penerbit: Bisa!Publishing 2016, Penulis: Muhammad Amien & Team


Misalkan: U = 3x² + 1
du/dx = 6x → dx = du/ 6x

∫ 3x √3x² + 1 dx

= ∫ (3x) U^1/2 . du/6x

= 1/2 ∫ U^1/2 du

= 1/2 . 2/3 . U^3/2 + C

= 1/3 (3x² + 1)√3x² + 1 + C

36. tuliskan 5 prediksi dalam bahasa inggris

Pelajaran: English
Kategori: making prediction
Pembahasan:

In making prediction, there are several ways to say it such as
- It'll definitely....
- It'll possibly...
- It might...
- It probably won't....
- It absolutely won't...

Here are the example:
- The robots will definitely be apart of our society in the future.
- The car will probably be faster than now.
- The gadget might have an invisible mode for security.
- The alien probably won't attack earth.
- We absolutely won't invade mars yet.
