14. buatlah dialog bahasa inggris, didalamnya harus ada 1. opinion 2. hope and plan3. feeling4. sympathy5. praise 6. proud​


Dialog:

A: Hey, how are you doing?

B: I'm doing alright. How about you?

A: I'm good, thank you. You know, I've been thinking about the current situation in our country. What's your opinion on it?

B: Well, honestly, it's quite concerning. There have been several challenges and issues that need to be addressed.

A: I completely agree. It's important for us to remain hopeful and come up with a plan to tackle these problems.

B: Absolutely. I believe that with unity and determination, we can work towards a better future.

A: That's a great mindset to have. How do you feel about the situation personally?

B: Honestly, it's a mix of emotions. There's a sense of frustration, but also a determination to make a difference and bring about positive change.

A: I understand how you feel. It's natural to have those emotions. I have sympathy for those who are directly affected by these challenges.

B: Thank you for your sympathy. It means a lot. But let's not forget that there are also many individuals and organizations working tirelessly to address these issues.

A: You're absolutely right. Their efforts deserve praise. It's remarkable how they continue to fight for a better future despite the obstacles.

B: I couldn't agree more. We should be proud of our fellow citizens who are making a difference and contributing to positive change in our country.

Note:

This dialogue showcases various elements, including expressing opinions, discussing hopes and plans, expressing feelings, showing sympathy, giving praise, and expressing pride.

31. dialog bahasa inggris tentang expressing a plan


Berikut adalah dialog Bahasa Inggris tentang expressing a plan (menyatakan rencana).

Ani: Hi, Dani, what's up?

Dani: Oh, hi, Ani, nothing, just chilling.

Ani: Hey, what is your plan for the holiday?

Dani: Well, I don't know, maybe I will go to my grandma in Bogor. How about you, what's your plan?

Ani: I have booked a ticket to Belitong island, it is a beautiful island with beach resorts.

Dani: Wow, I wish I can go with you.

Ani: My father has a friend there, he will take us around good places in Belitong.

Pembahasan

Dialog di atas tentang expressing a plan (menyatakan rencana) bila kita terjemahkan menjadi:

Ani: Hai, Dani, apa kabar?

Dani: Oh, hai, Ani, tidak apa-apa, hanya bersantai.

Ani: Hei, apa rencana liburanmu?
