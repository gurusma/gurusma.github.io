12. The tourist.......(leave) the town


Jawaban:

The tourist leaves the town (simple present tense)

The tourist left the town (past tense)

The tourist is leaving the town (present continuous tense)

The tourist has left the town (present continuous tense)

The tourist will leave the town (simple future tense)

Penjelasan:

Pada soal di atas tidak dijelaskan tense yang digunakan. Selain itu tidak ada pula keterangan waktu yang menjadi ciri dari suatu tense. Tetapi, yang diperhatikan adalah penggunaan auxiliary verb pada kalimat tersebut.

Pada soal di atas, subject (the tourist) adalah orang ketiga tunggal. Sehingga auxiliary verb yang digunakan adalah is, was, atau has.

Contoh: The tourist left the town yesterday (past tense)

The tourist is leaving the town now (present continuous tense)

The tourist has left the town for two hours.

I live in Yogyakarta, the city is well known for its cultural heritage. There you can find many public transportation system such as TransJogja, Becak, Delman, Angkot, Ojek, etc. But today I will tell you a story about becak, my favorite transport system.


Becak is the kind of vehicle which fits two passengers, sometimes more (depending on the size of a person and size of the becak itself) some becak is bigger than the other. On top of the passengers seat, there is a protection made out of canopy that will protect you from the sun. Moreover, there is also a transparent plastic sheet that comes down over the front, to cover when the rain comes.


In Yogyakarta, there are two types of becak these days. The manual and the half-motor. Prices for manual definitely will be more expensive as you are riding based on the becak driver’s energy, while the half-motor is using machine.


Usually people will bargain before get in to the becak as sometimes driver can rip you off with 2 to 3 times more expensive price, so be careful and be smart.

26. Town A to Town B is 50 km away From town A, I drove the car around 100km/hr to town B What is the time did i go?


Jawab:

half an hour (1/2 hour)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

If the distance is 50 km and the speed is 100 km/h, then:

time = distance / speed

time = 50 / 100

time = 1/2 hour

So, the time we need to drive to Town B is half an hour.

Pembahasan

Untuk menjawab soal ini, perhatikan terjemahan pada setiap opsi.

A. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in a small town in Sumatra. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di sekitar danau di sebuah kota kecil di Sumatra)

B. Dion plans to go to a nice place in small town in Sumatra around a lake. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di sebuah kota kecil di Sumatra sekitar sebuah danau)

C. Around a lake Dion plans to go to a nice place in a small town in Sumatra. (= Sekitar sebuah danau Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di sebuah kecil di Sumatra)

D. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in Sumatra in a small town. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah sekitar sebuah danau di Sumatra di sebuah kota kecil)

E. Dion plans to go to a nice place in Sumatra in a small town around a lake. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di Sumatra di sebuah kota kecil sekitar sebuah danau)
