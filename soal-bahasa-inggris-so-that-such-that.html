9. Percakapan 4 orang dalam bahasa inggris bertemakan hari kemerdekaan dengan menggunakan so that such that


Pembahasan:

Pertama, kakak akan menjelaskan tentang so ... that dan such ... that terlebih dahulu.

So dan such digunakan untuk memberi penekanan "sangat" pada kualitas seseorang atau suatu benda. sedangkan that yang dalam konteks ini berarti "sehingga" menunjukkan bahwa penekanan kualitas seseorang atau suatu benda menyebabkan sesuatu terjadi. Jadi, So .. that dan such ... that adalah conjunction yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan sesuatu terjadi karena kualitas seseorang atau suatu benda.

Pola kalimat:

So + adj/adv + that

Contoh kalimat: The novel is so interested that many people want to buy it.

Such + noun + that

Contoh kalimat: It is such a nice book that i like to read it over and over again.

berikut contoh dialog 4 orang yang so ... that dan such ... that bertema hari kemerdekaan

A: The independence day is coming soon.

B: Yeah, i can't wait to celebrate it.

D: Me too. It is such a special day for our country that it is set as the national holiday.

C: Oh, Don't forget about the competition, too. Our school holds the Independence Day Race every year.

B: Yeah, you're right. i want to join the tug of war competition. How about you guys?

C: i want to join the sack race.

A: I want to join the egg and spoon race. i almost won it last year.

D: i want to join the cracker eating competition. I always win that competition.

A: No wonder, You're so tall that you can reach the cracker easily.

D: Yeah. That's the benefit of being tall.

C: Forget about the competition for now, guys. We have to practice for the flag ceremony now.

B: You're right.

A: Yeah, let's gather with the others now, Mr. Ahmad will be mad if we are late.

13. Dialog Such That, So That dan so secara singkat tentang Kelas


Pembahasan:

Pertama, kakak akan menjelaskan tentang so ... that, such ... that, dan so terlebih dahulu.

So dan such digunakan untuk memberi penekanan "sangat" pada kualitas seseorang atau suatu benda. sedangkan that yang dalam konteks ini berarti "sehingga" menunjukkan bahwa penekana kualitas seseorang atau suatu benda menyebabkan sesuatu terjadi. Jadi, So .. that dan such ... that adalah conjunction yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan terjadinya sesuatu yang disebabkan oleh kualitas seseorang atau suatu benda.

Pola kalimat:

So + adj/adv + that

Contoh kalimat: She is so beautiful that many boys like her.

Such + noun + that

Contoh kalimat: It is such a nice car that many people buy it.

So + adj/adv

Contoh kalimat: This teddy bear is so cute.

A: Really? Welcome to our school. What do you think about your new class?

A: You're right. That's because of our class rule, there's a fine for those who litter. The fine for littering is so expensive that all of our classmates don't litter. what do you think about our classmates?

B: Oh i see. I think our classmates are such nice people that i can adapt easily.

A: Glad to hear that. By the way, l want to go to the canteen. Do you want to come along?

22. Apa bedanya so-that dengan such-that? Kapan pakai so-that dalam kalimat? Kapan pakai such-that dalam kalimat?


So ____ that digunakan ketika kalimatnya mengunakan kata adjective (sifat).
------ so+(adj.)+that
Contoh kalimat: She is so annoying that I want to kick her.

Such ____ that digunakan ketika kalimatnya menggunakan kata adverb (keterangan)
------ such+(adverb)+that
Contoh kalimat: She is such a pretty girl that I think she is the prettiest girl I've known.

Untuk membedakan adjective dan adverb, biasanya adj. terdiri dari satu kata dan adverb terdiri dari dua kata.
Contoh:
Adj: diligent
Adverb: diligent girl

32. Buat soal pilihan ganda so that and such that 10 soal


Explanation:

Such … that is followed by a noun phrase (can be singular or plural).

For example, She is such a genius girl that she always wins a science olympiad.

So … that is followed by an adjective or adverb or determiner (such as many, much, and little).

For example, your hair is so wonderful that everyone feels jealous.
