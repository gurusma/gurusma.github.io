37. future tense tobe going to adalah dan contoh (+) (-) dan (?)


Simple future tense dengan “going+to” untuk menyatakan intention (rencana dimasa depan yang sudah dipikirkan sebelumnya).

(+) He is going to go to French to continue his study
(-) He isn't going to go to French to continue his study
(?) Is he going to go to French to continue his study?

38. Contoh dialog bahasa Inggris i am going to and i would like to


Here is the example of dialogue using going to and would like to.

W: Dude, I heard you're going to change your major at university, is it true?

Y: Yeah, Wamberto, I don't think I can't take it anymore, dealing with chemical engineering stuff.

W: So, you just give up the whole year here?

Y: I'm afraid so. I feel lost here, my passion is not here. I'm going to take another entrance test for English Literature.

W: Really? You switch to social subject? C'mon, dude, think it over. Think of your parents.

Y: Well, I have told them. I just wanna live with my passion. I would like to be a writer or screenwriter.

W: OK, I wish you luck, Yuri.

Y: Thanks, mate. I'll pay a visit sometime later.

Penjelasan:

Frase 'going to' dan 'would like to' memiliki arti dan fungsi yang berbeda satu sama lain.

'Going to' artinya adalah 'akan' dan berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan rencana untuk melakukan sesuatu di suatu waktu yang akan datang.'Would like to' artinya adalah 'ingin' dan berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan ambisi atau keinginan.
