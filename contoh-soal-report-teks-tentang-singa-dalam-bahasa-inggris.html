14. deskripsi tentang singa dalam bahasa inggris


The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the five big cats in the genus Panthera and a member of the family Felidae. With some males exceeding 250 kg (550 lb) in weight,[4] it is the second-largest living cat after the tiger. Wild lions currently exist in sub-Saharan Africa and in Asia (where an endangered remnant population resides in Gir Forest National Park in India) while other types of lions have disappeared from North Africa and Southwest Asia in historic times. Until the late Pleistocene, about 10,000 years ago, the lion was the most widespread large land mammal after humans. They were found in most of Africa, across Eurasia from western Europe to India, and in the Americas from the Yukon to Peru. The lion is a vulnerable species, having seen a major population decline in its African range of 30–50% per two decades during the second half of the 20th century. Lion populations are untenable outside designated reserves and national parks. Although the cause of the decline is not fully understood, habitat loss and conflicts with humans are currently the greatest causes of concern. Within Africa, the West African lion population is particularly endangered.

Animals which commonly live in a group and dense forests or savannas which have lots of food are Lion. A group of lion consists of one male and many females. The male is the leader of the group who keeps the male lions and hunting areas, while the female lions have a task to look for food by hunting together.

Lion is often referred to call as the king of the jungle because lion is a very wild and malignant animal. Their big body and sinister face coupled with a long mane makes his enemy fears against them.

Their Weight can reach 225kg for males and 150 kg for females. In general, lion age could reach 10 to 15 years in the wild but if they are kept in a cage, they could reach 20 years old. This is because the lions kept alive by human, their foods will be guaranteed.

When compared with other big cats, lions look more powerful. However, lions cannot climb a tree like most other cats. Their mane is used to protect them from his enemies during a fight. A fight between a male lion is very common in the wild. They fight over territory and females. For those who win will have the hunt region and group, while the loser will leave the region.

Si Raja Hutan

Hewan yang hidup secara berkelompok ini biasa hidup di hutan-hutan yang lebat atau padang-padang safana yang banyak terdapat makanan. Singa memilki kelompok yang terdiri dari satu jantan dan banyak betina. Sang jantan merupakan pemimipin kelompok yang menjaga singa betina dan daerah perburuannya. Sedangkan singa betina bertugas mencari makanan dengan berburu bersama-sama.

Singa sering disebut sebagai raja hutan karena singa adalah hewan yang sangat buas dan ganas. Badanya yang besar dan wajahnya yang seram ditambah lagi dengan surai yang panjang membuat musuhnya ketakutan saat melawan mereka.

Berat singa dapat mencapai 225kg untuk jantan dan 150kg untuk betina. pada umum nya usia singa bisa mencapai 10 sampai 15 tahun di alam liar nya, tapi jika dipelihara usianya bisa mencapai 20 tahun. hal ini dikarenakan singa-singa yang hidup dipelihara oleh manusia kebutuhan makanannya akan terjamin.

Jika dibandingkan dengan kucing besar yang lain, singa terliat lebih perkasa atau lebih kuat. Namun singa tidak bisa memanjat pohon seperti kebanyakan kucing lain. Surai pada singa jantan berfungsi untuk melindungi dirinya dari musuhnya saat terjadi perkelahian. Perkelahian antar singa jantan sangat sering terjadi di alam liar untuk saling memperebutkan wilayah dan betina.
