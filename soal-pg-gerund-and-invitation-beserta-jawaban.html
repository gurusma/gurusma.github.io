38. soal gerund dan infinitve beserta jawabannya


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: Englsh
Kategori: gerund vs infintive
Pembahasan:

Fill in the gaps with gerund or infintive
- The team decides ______________(sell) their star player as his action begins __________(cause) disharmony in it.
- I love _____________(play) football but I kinda loathe _____________(watch) it.
- She doesn't mind ___________(do) housework but she doesn't want _________(do) it everyday.
- They weren't prepared _________(have) a baby although they fancy _______(take) care of her.

answer:
- to sell; to cause
- playing ; playing
- doing ; to do
- to have ; taking care
